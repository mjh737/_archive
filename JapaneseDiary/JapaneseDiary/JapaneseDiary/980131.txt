Saturday 31st January
Got up really early and had a huge breakfast in true Kawase-san style. I tried on their spare ski boots but they were all too small so when we eventually got to the ski slope, I had to hire some with skis and sticks as well, which, of course, the Kawase’s paid for.  The first time we came down I couldn’t do it at all and kept falling over but we realised that the binding was loose so I had to go and get them tightened.  After that, it was easier and I was pretty damned good.  Rich and I stayed there until 4pm (on the expert slopes) expect for lunch, paid for by the Kawase’s.  When we finished we drove to a restaurant and had a good meal, courtesy of the Kawase’s. We talked about going night snow-boarding, but it didn’t happen cos Richard was a bit tired.  We went back to the condo and fell asleep in front of the TV after more food and a game of chess.
