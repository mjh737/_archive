Friday 20th March
Happy Birthday Kate!  Spent the morning studying and reading the papers that Dad bought me.  Caught the train to Hakusan with Rich and after walking through Hakusan Shrine and going to the bank we ended up checking out the department stores in Furumachi but didn’t buy anything.  We had a milkshake in McDonalds before hiking over the bridge into Bandai to meet Balàzs and Miyako at 5pm.  We booked our tickets to go to Sado Island next Wednesday and went to meet Andy, Rachel and Seiko at the station.  We headed off to the American Diner as usual to take advantage of the half price pitchers of beer.  I had the cheapest food on the menu, a chicken burger, which was big and delicious.  We all had a couple of pitchers before going next door to do karaoke.  I can’t remember anything about that but afterwards, Richard pushed Balàzs into a fountain.  Balàzs turned round and thought it was me.  He charged at me with the ‘I’m going to kill you’ face that he donned before when Richard was picking on Miyako.  He grabbed my arms and I panicked and head-butted him before he had chance to do whatever he was going to do.  I explained the actual cause of his wet shoes and things seem to be okay.  Richard admitted full liability for the scuffle.  We went to a club called Ezra in Furumachi which is the first time I have been to a club in Japan.  It was quite good really – small and not very busy but enjoyable because it was mainly just us on the dance floor.  We stayed until closing which must have been 3am and then everyone went back to Miyako’s except Rich and I who went to the station and chatted to the homeless people on the station, buying them several coffees.  I caught the first train home and crawled into bed at 6am.
