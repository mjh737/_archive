Thursday 30th October
Went to a Law School Japanese lesson at 8.45.  At 10.30 I was supposed to have my first lesson without R & B but I couldn’t be arsed so I went and played pool with R & B, Tera (my tutor) and Yoshi (R’s).  I won again of course.
At 12 o’clock we met the saucy girls again who taught us a few Jappo swear words which, according to my dictionary can be translated as ‘don’t be a nuisance’ and such like.
I came home after that and Mrs Saiki took me to see this guy called Naoki who had a diving accident at Nigata uni 10 yrs ago which left him paralysed from the neck down.  He speaks English pretty good like what I do but I spent half the time talking to his father in Japanese about our mutual admiration of mountains.  On the way home, I mentioned my love of pizzas so Mrs S bought me one.  Feeling better about Jap today following a good, dictionary-less, conversation with my above-mentioned host Mum.
