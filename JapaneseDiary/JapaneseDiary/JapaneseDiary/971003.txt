Friday 3rd October
There was nobody here when I got up so I had to work out what breakfast was for me – not easy when there are a dozen different bowls of various sashimi etc.  When I left the house, I decided to pump the bike tyres up but accidentally half emptied it instead so had to cycle to the station a bit awkwardly (Is that spelt right? – I’ve forgotten English already!).  I met Balàzs and Rich and then the other two. We spent the day in Niigata, went to a noodle restaurant for lunch (where I had my first lager since arriving) and went up the “Rainbow Tower” - very high, very “spinning” but very “having a good view”.  I bought ‘The Firm’, Nihongo de and a nice bag for ¥2000 with which I am very pleased.
