Wednesday 6th May
Got up at 9.30 with, to my astonishment, merely a mild hangover.  I remember feeling quite ill as I walked home last night.  I spent most of the day being in love with the image of last night’s perfect girl which I have create in my mind.  Tomorrow I will phone her and, no doubt, get rejected.  I went to Ushi san’s house and taught Ayumu how to say ‘moustache’.  At least, I tried but after the 1283rd repetition, I realised that it was never going to sink in.  Yoshimi, the girl, had a similar problem with the word ‘fridge’.  When I got home, Mr Saiki cooked something and referred to it as ‘dinner’.  However I believe now that there must have been an error in my translation because it seemed rather un-dinneresque to me.  I walked Pao and came up for an early night.
