Tuesday 9th June
I finished ‘The Final Judgement’ and did nothing else all day until I got ready for my date with Yūmi.  She was half an hour late and, in that time, I consumed 3 cans of beer and realised how much I like this girl.  When she finally did turn up I was even more pleased to see her than I would have been had she been on time and besides she looked beautiful.  However, conversation was hard today, probably because I had only thought about discussing one particular topic.  So I had a few beers and, grabbing the bull by the horns, declared my deepest feelings; so well I think, that for a Japanese person, she came pretty close to understanding.  However, she was silent on the issue so, having embarrassed us both, I suggested we go outside and sit by the river in the dark.  We did, kissed a lot, chatted a lot, esp about her boyfriends, and then I left her making a late night appointment for tomorrow.  I caught the last train and was in bed by 12.15.
