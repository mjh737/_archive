Monday 29th December
Host Uncle picked me up at 10 o’clock and we drove half an hour to his house for coffee with his wife and parents.  Then we went to the Onsen (hot spring) and took our clothes off and sat in the hot water together – quite disturbing.  Then we went home but straight out to a Japanese restaurant for lunch.  He then took me to an old samurai barracks kind of place and made me model for him – yet more disturbing.  We returned home and then went to a petrol station together – cool!!  At 4.30 we went to a tiny old bar run by two old women.  There were two or three other people there and soon we were all drinking and chatting until eventually we got around to the karaoke.  We all went back to Uncles house for more beer and a little food.  It was quite a good fluent kind of chat. I stayed over in the guest room.
