Wednesday 31st December
Got up late and did nothing all day apart from watch TV, read, go to post office and to the petrol station with my host father.  We had a special meal which is the equivalent of our Christmas Turkey.  It was horrible Japanese stuff and I had to go and buy chocolate and beer afterwards.  At 11.30pm, I went with Mr and Mrs Saiki to the shrine at Hakusan but in the usual Saiki manner we were five minutes late seeing the New Year in but still I queued up for 20 minutes to make a wish at the shrine.  Afterwards we got food and then held a dried squid at the end of a 10ft bamboo cane, the purpose of which I am not entirely sure.  We watched a strange kind of float being shaken and carried along by a couple of hundred Japs in dressing gowns and then made our way home.
