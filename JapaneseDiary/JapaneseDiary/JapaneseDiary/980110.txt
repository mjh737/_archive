Saturday 10th January
Spent the day downstairs.  Breakfast was at 11 o’clock and I was chatting to Mr and Mrs Saiki as usual when suddenly Mr Saiki completely ignored me and Mrs Saiki’s face changed.  I then realised that Akihiro had a huge earring.  They were visibly angry but typically Japanese about it.  At 4pm I met Balàzs and Rich and we hung around in our suits, getting the eye, for a bit until we went to the law dept party at Niigata Washington Hotel.  I chatted up a legal exec and chatted to a 72 year old bloke who started learning English 5 years ago but is now fluent.  I spoke to Miho briefly and she reckons she’s going to write to me tomorrow – I won’t get too excited.  We moved on to the American Diner for more beer.  I went (and took Rich with me) and sat next to two Jap girls, both of whom were okay looking.  After chatting them up for half an hour in Japanese we ascertained that they were boy friendless, about 20 and maybe nurses.  We arranged a meeting for tomorrow at 2.00.
