Tuesday 30th June 
Woke up with a slight hangover and, having said my goodbyes, I made my way back to Kobari.  I would have said ‘home’ rather than ‘Kobari’ but that would have been stupid because my home is in England and I can’t go there today...oh actually, I can!!  And I will!!  Spent the day packing.  In the afternoon Richard’s host Mother came round and gave me £150...which was nice.  I sat around for a few hours before dinner.  After that Tatsuru, Meiki and Ayako came round for tea briefly.  After that was my most boring ever hour in Japan.  All packed I sat and waited for my lift to the station.  When  I was finally there, loads of people had turned up to see me off – Tatsuru & Meiko, Rich, Ayako (who gave me an earring), host family, Daisuke.  It was quite a moving experience and my host Mum cried.  She and Ayako stayed on until about ten seconds before teh train departed at 11.30pm.
