We had a penalty shoot-out competition which I won (against Andy, in goal, who’s the only one who plays properly) with a 100% success rate – (Mat, take note!). Cycled to Kobari station for the first time – I think I’ll cycle all the way tomorrow.  Met Balàzs’s host brother and friends, who were all very nice, and went to a bar for food and biiru.  We played Fizu Bazu (Fizz Buzz) and had too much to drink.  Rich and I left early (about 10pm) and went home to bed because we’re good.
