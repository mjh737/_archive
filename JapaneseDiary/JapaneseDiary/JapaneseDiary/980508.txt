Friday 8th May
Although I had every intention of doing my dissertation all day today I only managed to do two hours.  The rest of the day I spent reading the Richard North Patterson book which Andy lent me – it is very good.  I also wrote to Katy and phoned Mrs Usui to rearrange my lesson next week in respect of the fact that I have a date.  I had a very nice dinner, at least much better than the uncooked noodles I had for lunch.  I walked Pao and, when I got back, watched a good drama on TV which I understood at least 90% of.  Now I am once again alone in my room with Mr Beam, that’s beam not bean.  It’s 10 o’clock but probably still bed time – goodnight.
