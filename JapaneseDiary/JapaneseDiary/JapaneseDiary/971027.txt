Monday 27th October
Woke up at nine but my lecture wasn’t until 2.30 so I just dossed around (in English) for a few hours.  Got a letter from Katy which was nice.  Wrote back in my typical maniacal style.  First lecture was with Nakamura Sensei who is quite a cool, multi-lingual kind of a guy.  Learnt no Japanese but did learn a few Hungarian proverbs and received Nakamura Sensei’s rendition of an old Russian love song.  The next lecture lasted until 6 o’clock.  1¾ hours of Japanese law in Japanese, none of which I understood.  When I got home I was so tired from the Japanese, I hardly said a word to the Saiki’s but was too tired to come upstairs until 8.30.  I just vegged in front of the TV.  It’s now 10 o’clock and about time I started revising for tomorrow’s kanji test which I really can’t face in my present state.  Incidentally, most of you may know how hot I always am.  Well, you’ll be surprised to learn that I am currently wearing a t-shirt and two thick padded shirts and am still cold!!
