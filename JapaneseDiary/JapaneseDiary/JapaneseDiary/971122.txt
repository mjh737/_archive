Studied a bit but went into Niigata to meet B at 3.00.  Had a couple of games of Sega before searching for a bar.  3 hours later, we found a bar and managed to get one beer in before meeting B’s host brother along with 3 friends.  Went to a Nabe restaurant and met Hiroshi’s girlfriend who was an absolute delight.  We all drank heavily before staggering on to a karaoke bar.  I sang a few Beatles songs and a Japanese ‘popular’ ‘music’ song.  It was very good fun.  When we left I (obviously) beat the others at Sega Rally a couple of times.  We moved on to another bar for more beer, by this stage, really didn’t need it.  We got a taxi home and parted company with Kyoko, Hiroshi’s girlfriend.  I went back to Balązs’s house to stay the night and we both fell into drunken slumbers (separately) in no time at all.
