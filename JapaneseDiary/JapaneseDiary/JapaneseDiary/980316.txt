Monday 16th March
In the morning Mrs Saiki said she was driving in to Niigata at 1.00pm so I could have a lift since I was meeting the boys at 2.00pm.  At 12.50pm I went downstairs where she announced that the reason she was going to town was to give Akihiro a lift to the station because he was going to Tokyo to see the Rolling Stones – the very same concert that I told her I wanted to go to but couldn’t afford to take Akihiro as well and had no one else to go with – thanks a lot!  Anyway, the boys were fine.  We had a few goes on Victory Lap before doing a bit of shopping.  We sat in a coffee shop for an hour just chatting before going to the American Diner at 5pm for happy hour where we remained until late, becoming steadily inebriated.  I got the train back at I don’t know what time and crawled into bed even later with a fear of waking up hungover.
