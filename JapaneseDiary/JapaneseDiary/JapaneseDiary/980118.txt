Sunday 18th January
Woke at ten and tried to sneak a breakfast in without Mrs Saiki knowing but she came in and force fed me some green and yellow stuff.  I decided to cycle in to Niigata with a large shopping list but only bought a black pen and some kanji cards.  I cycled back again (about 40 mins each way) and got soaked.  In the afternoon I got fed some cake-type things despite having just stuffed my face with my secret sandwich stash in my room.  I finished Steinbeck no.6 and studied kanji for the rest of the day and night.  Tea was late, about 8.30am but it was gyōza which I love.  After that I watched the tv Meiki restoration drama before coming back and studying until 2am.  Mrs Saiki showed me an advert in the paper for a £50 3 day cruise to Hokkaidō which I and the others will probably take her up on.  Mum and Dad phoned 7.00pm.  I miss them a lot but will see them in about 6 weeks.
