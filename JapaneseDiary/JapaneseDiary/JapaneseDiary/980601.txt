One month from now I will be in England.  The question mark reminds me that I should read this weekend’s entries to see if I had a good weekend.  (Five minutes later.)  I did.  Anyway, today I went to college, despite it being a holiday, and met Tera, my personal tutor who I am supposed to see everyone week but have only seen one before this year.  Anyway, as usual conversation was not forthcoming so we plated pool for two hours instead.  This was okay and I’m pretty food if I concentrate.  I came home and hung around for a couple of hours before getting ready to go for my date with Yūmi.  Despite her being the most beautiful girl in the world, I wasn’t looking forward to the meeting very much.  This was due to the fact that I hadn’t seen her for a couple of weeks and when I first met her I told her we would just be friends.  Being ‘just good friends’ in a different language is not easy.  Anyway, as soon as I saw her I remembered why I should have looked forward to it – she is absolutely lovely.  She suggested we go to the American Diner where I had fajitas and she, a burger.  After the meal, she paid (cool!) and we sat in the bar area, me buying the drinks. Anyway her counselling gave me the idea to do the do the opposite of whatever she said.  She said don’t make a move on Yūmi because she would not be interested.
