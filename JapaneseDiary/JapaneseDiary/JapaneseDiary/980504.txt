Monday 4th May
We played games until late and then Andy made me a huge fry up which was delicious.  We played cards for an hour and then decided to go to the beach to watch the sun come up.  When we got there we couldn’t resist having a swim which was cold but great fun although perhaps a little dangerous – the current was very strong.  After that we decided to go back and shower and eat another fry up.  After that we slept for four hours and after that, we went back to the beach for the day.  It was great – blue sea and blue sky, sandy beach and burning sun.  Andy and I, like a couple of big restless kids, had a long jump competition which he just about won but then again he is twice my height.  Then I decided we should dig a hole around Rachel so that she was left on a 10 feet high Rachel-shaped mound of sand.  We failed but it was about two feet deep.  We came home and I had dinner before coming up to finish my new book and get an early night.
