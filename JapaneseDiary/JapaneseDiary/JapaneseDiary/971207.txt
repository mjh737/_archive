Sunday 7th December
Got up at 7.00 and cycled to Rich’s house.  I got a bit lost due to the inadequacy of Japanese maps but after 90 mins and asking for help, I got there.  R’s host brother took us to the mountain I tried to climb on my own before but this time we made it, although we did get lost.  It was about 900 metres and quite hard work.  At the top, there was quite a bit of snow which made the journey down somewhat treacherous.  However, after slipping, I realised that it was much quicker to go down on my back and, besides, it was great fun.  Host brother picked us up again and, back at Rich’s we had a nice meal before going home in his host fathers Jeep heavily laden with bike & beer that his host mother insisted I have.  This evening me and Mrs Saiki drunk it and I spoke fluent Japanese for a couple of hours – don’t ask me how but somedays I can do it but most days I rarely understand anything.
