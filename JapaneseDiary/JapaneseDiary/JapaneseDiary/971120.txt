Thursday 20th November
Hunt wrote to me – got it today – short and crap but nice to be remembered.  Got back from college at 12.00 and had a little sleep cos I was feeling ‘dismal’ – what a nice word – cycled into Niigata to meet Rich.  Had lunch at the Rāmen Place and went to Next 21 to see Pygmalion in English which was cool.  Afterwards went to American Restaurant with Obligatory Maz Monroe & Jimmy Dean photos.  Had fajitas and a beer before moving on with A & R and Seiko & Sister Yuuko who was nice.  Had a few more beers before trying to cycle home a new way.  It takes half hour usually so when I had cycled what I thought was the most direct route for 30 mins and not seeing my house, I gave up and cycled back to Niigata and then home.  I got home an hour and a half after leaving (1am.  Checked map – when I turned round, I was 100 meters from the house!! What a fool!! So, good sense of direction but lacking in self confidence!
