Friday 1st May
I shall be leaving Niigata next month.  Hooray!  But it’s still 61 days to go.  Boo!  Today was another of those being-in-my-own-world-hangover days which just seem to pass by without any effort.  I got up at 10am and had toast for breakfast as usual.  Two hours later I was called down for a huge lunch and shortly after that I decided to cycle into town and buy myself a new book (fiction). It was a really hot day and as I cycled back I suddenly realised that I might as well just sit by the river in the sun for an hour.  So I did and it was so pleasant that I may well do the same thing tomorrow.  It’s Mrs Saiki’s birthday today and her friends came round to look at me.  We had a nice dinner (sashimi/sushi) and then Mrs Saiki and a male friend drank just under a quarter of a bottle of sake between them.  They were absolutely sloshed – it was so funny.  I finished off the remaining ¾ and came up here to read for a bit and wonder what all the fuss was about.
