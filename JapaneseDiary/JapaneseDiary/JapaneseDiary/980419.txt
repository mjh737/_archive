Sunday 19th April
I have now been here one day longer than I had yesterday which is encouraging.  Coincidentally there is also one day less to go.  Today I just sat around and did absolutely nothing with the assistance of a severe hangover.  I had salad for lunch and watched the end of a film called ‘Loser’ which probably would have been quite good had I seen it all.  Dinner was one of those diy-in-the-middle-of-the-table jobs which was quite enjoyable – I look forward to having another of Kester’s stone meals when I get home.  Mr Saiki asked me again if we have hot springs in England.  In fact I don’t think he has asked me this for at least a week.  I was actually thinking he had got round to learning that fact.  Straight after dinner Mrs Saiki went to her room, Mr Saiki went out and Aki-chan walked the dog.  So I’m up here again awaiting the 9 o’clock movie.
