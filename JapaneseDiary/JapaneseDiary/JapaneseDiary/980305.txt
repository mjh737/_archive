Thursday 5th March
Two days to go and Mum and Dad will be here.  I’m really looking forward to it but I can only imagine how I’m going to feel this time next week.  I was pretty gutted when my Tunbridge Wells friends left after two days in Cardiff.  Well at least there is only 4 months left.  Anyway I met Rich in town at 2 PM and we went to the travel agents to book the hotel in Tokyo for next week and the Sadō Island ferry.  She gave me the bill which was about £200 and I didn’t have enough so I had to run to the bank which was embarrassing and very un-Japanese.  I bought a great magic trick and a pocket version of Jap chess before returning home.  I walked the despised creature of Satan before coming up to sort stuff out for my late night trip to Tokyo tomorrow night.
