23rd June 
Got up at 1.30am.  I’ve had a hideout seething hangover all day today.  At 3pm I managed to get showered and dressed to go and pick my bike up from the bike shop.  Unfortunately I had no money so I had to go into town first to the cash point.  I bumped into Balazs and Miyako and we have arranged to arrange a last trip to the diner on Thursday.  I went to the bike shop in Terao and the bloke brought out the bike with about eight new parts – it’s as good as new now and will probably cost a fortune (apparently he will send me the bill).  I came home and did nothing for a couple of hours before eating breakfast (? – dinner surely) alone.  I walked that most despicable creature before coming up to, well not really to do anything – my head still hurts.  I think I will put some j-pops on quietly and allow a beautiful Japanese lady to sing me to sleep.
