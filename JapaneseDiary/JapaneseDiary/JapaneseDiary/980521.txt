Thursday 21st May
Met Rich at uni and then went to town to buy bus tickets for our trip to Tokyo with Dom next Friday.  Came home, showered and went back out to meet the lovely Yūmi.  Today the train was really busy.  Japanese people filled all the seats and packed the aisle.  However, the seats either side of me were empty and no-one stood next to me.  This kind of blatant racism really makes me angry especially when another nearby seat becomes vacant and they sit down there.  Anyway, I met Yūmi and conversation was good.  We went to Magic Shitty and had a couple of beers and played pool.  She had never played before and was really crap.  This meant A) I looked like a pro and B) I could teach her.  Afterwards we got a burger in McDonalds and I managed to create a river of ketchup down my white t-shirt – nice.  She went home and I went home, miserable because she is so lovely and I will never see her again after I leave Japan in 41 days.
