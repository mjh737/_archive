Friday 6th March
Went down to find Mrs Saiki on her way out.  Decided this time to put my breakfast in the microwave and, to my surprise, it was okay/all right.  I had eight bags of rubbish in my room so I decided to dispose of them by cycling around the local (less than 5 minutes away) convenience store of which there are ‘conveniently’ eight and using their bins.  In the afternoon Akihiro gave me a Gameboy to play with.  I almost fainted at 1, hearing his voice and 2, the open show of friendship.  After dinner I walked Pao before having a jolly little chat to the Saikis.  At 9.30 I left home to catch the train into Niigata.  I walked to the bus depot and had to wait one hour for the bus.  I got on but unfortunately was accompanied by a fat, sweaty, hairy, out-of-date, smelly Japanese bloke whose snoring I had to withstand for the entire journey.  We finally pulled into Tokyo at 4.30am, having had no sleep and this is where I shall begin my account of…
