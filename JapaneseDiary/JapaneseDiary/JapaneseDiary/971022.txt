Wednesday 22nd October
The Jap lesson this morning was hideous.  I didn’t understand any of it.  I thought I’m not learning Jap here, I don’t understand and my host family don’t like me.  I’m gonna go home to England but after lunch I had a Jap lesson which I understood so I felt better.  This evening I seemed to be getting on better with the Saiki’s so I am generally a bit happier and more keen to master this bitch of a language.  It’s so different from English and European languages.  You have to construct sentences backwards, so if the sentence is long it’s a nightmare to do it in your head.  I’m not homesick here really but I do miss TW, Cardiff and toilets which don’t have automatic hand-washing facilities built into the flushing system.  Oh, and I miss cashpoint machines which don’t have cartoon Jap women which speak and bow to you all the time.
