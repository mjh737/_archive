Monday 25th May
Woke up at Misa’s with terrible hayfever.  It took them three hours to have a shower and get ready.  I sat around looking at photos and not much else.  We went to Neguro or somewhere and went to the bank for Misa and a mobile phone shop for Yūri.  Finally, at 2.30 I had my first real meal since yesterday at 1.00.  It was the thinnest pizza I had ever seen with the most ridiculous volume/price ratio I have ever encountered.  Fortunately Misa paid.  After that we got the train to Ikebukuro but Yūri became ill due to her large baby-filled stomach and we had to find a doctors where she could lay down.  Misa and I went and had a look round the local shops for about an hour before getting a milkshake from McDonalds for lunch/dinner.  We said our goodbyes and phoned Yūri to do the same but I couldn’t hear anything on Misa’s little phone. Anyway, I left Misa and waited for the bus, hoping for another attractive travelling companion.  When the bus came I got on, sat down and waited.  Whether or not my travelling companion was attractive or not I don’t know but he was one of the most talkative people I have ever met.  He couldn’t speak English at all but insisted on speaking to me constantly in Japanese for 4 hours until he reluctantly departed.  I guess he was quite a nice guy really but due to the past few days sleep deprivation, all I really wanted to do was lay back and sleep.  I let him go at Nagaoka and fortunately no phone no’s were exchanged.  I got off the bus at Niigata station and had to wait half hour for a train so I bought some sandwich making materials for my room in the interim.  I finally got home at about 11.15, said Hello briefly to Mrs Saiki before escaping to my room for a good night’s sleep.
