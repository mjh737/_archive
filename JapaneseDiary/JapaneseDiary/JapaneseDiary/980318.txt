Wednesday 18th March
Studied and did little else until 2.00 before going to buy some stamps and envelopes on the way to my students house.  Taught for most of the time but had a game of Japanese chess with Ayumu and beat the little brat despite it being my second ever game and despite it being more difficult that building thermo-nuclear warheads from bits of old shoes.  I had a nice meal with them including these shiitake mushrooms which she’d filled with cheese – really nice.  Then I went on to my middle-aged students house which, in terms of difficulty is somewhere between nuclear bombs and Japanese chess.  Cycled home just below the sound barrier due to my new streamlined physique and athletic fitness level.  Here I’ve got a lot done over the last hour or so – letters and stuff.  Now (10.30) I shall study a little before sleeping.  I am meeting Rich tomorrow but, after that have no plans until my next student session in two weeks.
