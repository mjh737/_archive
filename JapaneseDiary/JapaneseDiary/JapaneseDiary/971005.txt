Sunday 5th October
Met Rich and Balazs in town at 11 o’clock and we explored more of the city centre, i.e. the amusement arcades.  I bought the Japanese version of Alice in Wonderland which I’ve started translating.  I wonder if I’ll ever finish it.  I’ve not read it in English.  We had noodles for lunch including four whole tiny pieces of chicken but I got a beer so I was happy.  We had average home-made curry for dinner but, after that, Mr Saiki and I sat at the table for 3 hours and read the encyclopaedia.  When I finally got away at 9:30 I went to my room to study Kanji and read “Alice” and basically sort my room out which I hadn’t really done at that stage.  Tomorrow is a college meeting day to choose modules.
