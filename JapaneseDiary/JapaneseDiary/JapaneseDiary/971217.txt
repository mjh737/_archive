Wednesday 17th December
Went out on my bike early to search for the bike lock and glove which I lost on Sunday.  After an hour I found the lock!  But I couldn’t find my glove.  I went to meet Miho for lunch at 12 but thought I’d been stood up until she arrived 5 mins late – with a friend – so we had to go to lunch, all three of us.  Fortunately, her friend, Yukio, had a lecture so Miho and I went back to her apartment.  She gave me a pair of gloves so all my worries are over!  She showed me her English notebook and while she was in the kitchen I had a look.  She rushed back in, in a panic and at the same time I saw my name in the book!  She had planned our whole conversation with all permutations in English but the only one I saw was ‘Sorry, I’m late’ so she planned her tardiness!!  When I returned home I had Pao duty to attend to and, with great difficulty, I have just finished studying for tomorrow’s big end of term kanji test.
