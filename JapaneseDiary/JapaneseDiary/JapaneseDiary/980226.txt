Thursday 26th February
Did nothing again all day.  Lunch time I cycled up to ‘Save on’, the local convenience store in order to buy some lunch.  I came back and had a nice boiled egg.  Balàzs phoned at 3pm and I agreed to meet him on Saturday with his English host father, Mike, who is here for a couple of weeks.  I watched a bit of TV with the Saikis before leaving at 10 o’clock to catch the train into Niigata to meet Rich because we were catching the moonlight train to Tokyo to meet Minamikata Sensei tomorrow for a guided tour.  We stocked up on food and drink for the journey and caught the train at 11.30pm.  The journey was quite boring but we could not sleep.  We finally crawled off the train at Shinjuku in Tokyo at 5.00am but that’s another day…
