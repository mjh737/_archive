Sunday 25th January
Got up at the crack of dawn again and after breakfast and tidying our rooms we got on the coach at about 9.00 and departed.  Half an hour later we stopped to look around some famous Japanese doctors birthplace.  He discovered the cure for Yellow Fever and then died of it a month later – cool!  On the way back to Niigata I played a great game of chess with Rich and won obviously.  When we got back, R & I did some e-mailing before going home which took ages because we had our ski bags.  I spent a few hours in the living room with the Saikis’ before dinner.  Afterwards I walked the animal, unpacked my stuff from the weekend and studied a little before going to bed and reading me Xth Steinbeck novel, Totilla Flat.
