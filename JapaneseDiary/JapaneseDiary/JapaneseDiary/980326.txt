Friday 26th March
I didn’t speak to a soul all day until dinner at 7.00pm but little was said even then.  I felt very alone and unhappy today.  All day I just wanted to write my diary so I could psychologically tick off one more of the 97 days left to go.  I read For Whom the Bell Tolls and my world history a bit, studied kanji and even played the computer game that Akihiro gave me a few weeks ago.  I can’t wait to get back to Cardiff to play real games with Kester.  I walked Pao after dinner for ages, just kind of ambling around moping and praying that I can go home 1st July and not 1st August.  When I got back I sat in the lounge for a bit and Minamikata Sensei phoned to invite me in for a chat about going home so tomorrow is the big day to decide on my parole conditions.  I will either be very very unhappy or averagely unhappy.  Now I am just pottering around and studying a bit but I’ll probably get an early tonight so I’m fresh for the big day.
