Friday 10th October
I’m sure we were supposed to go into college for a meeting today but none of us did so I guess it’s alright.  I cycled into Niigata which took an hour and met Balàzs and Rich at 3 o’clock from where we went to Sega World ‘which was nice’.  Balàzs’s party turned out to be quite cool.  I met a Canadian guy called Martin and his two students plus a really nice Nihonjin bloke called Hiroshi.  We’ve planned to go into Niigata next week and get pissed but the Nihonjin girls told me not to go, relaying the impression, through Martin, that perhaps his intended venue was not quite as reputable as one might like.  I can’t wait!  After the Paati I cycled home, somewhat over the limit but its quite safe since on-pavement’ cycling is encouraged.  If you’re thinking of coming to this God-forsaken country don’t bring your own electric toilet seat warmers as the toilets here are all equipped with them anyway – all, that is, apart from the ‘squat-and-shit’ variety (at which I need more practice).
