Wednesday 1st July 
The five and a half hour journey dragged on but it was only the tip of the iceberg.  I had to wait an hour for the Narita express, which took an hour and a half to get to the airport.  At the airport I waited another hour and a half before I could check in and then another hour and a half before I could board and then another hour for takeoff.  I sat next to an Oxford student called Sophie who had also just completed her year out.  The three films were okay but the journey dragged on and on.  I didn’t sleep and haven’t really since Saturday night.  At Heathrow, we taxied in alongside the England team.  After baggage reclaim found Kate, Dad and Mum (with a silly balloon) who took me, at long last, HOME.
