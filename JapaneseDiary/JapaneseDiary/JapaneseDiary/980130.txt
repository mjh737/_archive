Friday 30th January
Went for a bike ride to try and figure out where I went wrong when I cycled back from Niigata late at night that time two months ago.  I couldn’t find it at all.  I must have gone in completely the wrong direction.  Had insutanto rāmen for lunch and at 3.00pm Richard phoned to invite me skiing for the weekend.  Of course, I said yes and had to make hasty arrangements.  I went to the bank and took out £100 for emergencies but as it turned out I spent none.  I was picked up at 6.30 and we drove 2½ hours to Richard’s host family’s condo in the mountains at Akakura stopping only for a slap-up meal and toilet trips.  When we arrived we watched TV and Richard and his host Dad went for a bath while I did a Telegraph cryptic crossword.  We watched TV, played chess, chatted and drank beer until quite late before going to bed.
