Went down stairs and found Akihiro’s key on the table with my coffee cup so I assumed it was for me to borrow so I went into Niigata and got another one cut.  Whilst there I decided to buy a replacement for the other thing I lost- a bike lock.  So after that successful trip I came home for an hour, wrote to Grandad and then went to see my student.  Last week I phoned Mrs Usui to change it to Tuesday due to my date with Miss World tomorrow.  She phoned me at seven, yes seven, in the morning to tell me it’s just for two hours so I only taught from 4-6.  I told Mrs Usui about my date tomorrow and she said something which moved and encouraged me: “Matt, we think you are such a nice man, no Japanese person wouldn’t like you”.  So I’ve decided to be like I am at Usui’s (perfect, clean-cut Mother’s boy) rather than just be myself.  I bought some food on the way home, ate alone, walked Pao and came up.  Wish me luck for tomorrow!!!
