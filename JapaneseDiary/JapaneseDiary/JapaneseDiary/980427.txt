Monday 27th April
Today I just about managed to crawl out of bed at eight thirty and crawled to college for our lecture two hours later.  It was easy but I learnt tons of new words including ‘to graze oneself’ (very useful).  I went to the library for a bit and then Rich and I had rāmen before going in to town where I bought ‘Crime and Punishment’ in Japanese, in cassette form so I can listen to Japanese in England.  At the moment I can understand none of it.  At 8.00 Mrs Saiki came home and knocked on my door saying “Let’s eat out”.  I opened my mouth to reply but by that time she was outside in the car with the engine running.  I grabbed my socks and chased after her.  We went to a local and highly rated rāmen restaurant and had a superb meal.  Came back, walked Pao and came back up here to listen to me tape.
