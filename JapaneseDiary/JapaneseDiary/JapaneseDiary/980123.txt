Firday 23rd January
Arose at 6am and got ready for my skiing trip.  Mrs Saiki gave me a lift to the station and I met Balàzs at 8am.  The coach left at 9 o’clock and after hours of ludo, chess etc and lunch at some cheap rank restaurant arrived at the hotel (?!!) at about 1pm.  We did some really rubbish walk-skiing for 2 hours and Balàzs and I hated it – it was so boring.  When it was finally over we went in and had a brief sleep before dinner which was okay.  There was a kind of party with beer but we didn’t mingle.  Afterwards, we went to a room, just the five of us from England, and drank a bottle of whiskey.  We got very drunk and Balàzs fell asleep (later to vomit – for me to clear up).  Me, A & R, and Rich went outside and played soldiers and James Bond until late.
