Friday 14th November
Woke up at ten but felt terrible all day so I didn’t go to my lecture at 4.30 let alone to karaoke in the evening.  Studied most of the day and read loads of Marabou Stork Nightmares which is better than Trainspotting, cheers Morey.  Got a great letter from Katy which made me laugh so much I had to reply immediately.  When Mrs Saiki returned, she was surprised to find me in and even more surprised to find me in bed, ill.  She gave me some gruesome medicine and some soup and took my temperature before concluding I had a rare condition known as a cold – apparently it’s not too serious though.  After dinner, guess what, I studied, but in bed.  Mrs S came up an hour later with a drink for me but I had “nodded off”.  After that I couldn’t sleep so I read tons of grammar.  Balàzs phoned to find out where the hell I was and I kinda wished I’d gone out but still felt like death warmed up.  So I was good really.
