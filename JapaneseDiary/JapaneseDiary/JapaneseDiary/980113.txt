Tuesday 13th January
Went to college early and realised that I am rubbish in Japanese classes because they are so early in the morning.  Did some e-mailing and then went for Rāmen with Rich.  I got home just after lunch and spent the afternoon studying as usual.  Got a letter form Miho which was in Japanese, vague and slightly boring.  Went down to dinner at eight o’clock and there was a new pot of Marmite there.  I asked from whence it had come and Mrs Saiki said the other host brother.  He’d come home and she didn’t even bother telling me.  Anyway, he’s a little more talkative than the other one and he speaks English quite well.  We chatted about music and fashion and stuff and then, with Mrs Saiki, went to karaoke where I sang loads and well and Mrs Saiki thought I was absolutely ‘marvellous’. She bought me a couple of pints because I had no money and I quite enjoyed it.  At 11.30, I cam home to bed.
