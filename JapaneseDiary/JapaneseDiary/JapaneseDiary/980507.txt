Thursday 7th May
I awoke at 9.00am with a terror previously unsurpassed in intensity for today was the day that I had to make ‘the phone call’, a phone call to the most beautiful girl to whom I have ever spoken.  I knew that I would keep putting it off unless I set a time, so that I did – 3.06.  At the allotted time I made the call, having for the previous four hours consumed 6 Irish, well Kentucky, coffees and a few more without any coffee.  In fact they were more like Columbian whiskies than Irish coffees.  I also had spent an hour preparing all translations of every permutation of every sentence which might crop up.  It was an answerphone.  I hung up and spent the next 20 mins drinking and planning a small speech requesting a return call.  At just after 6pm the phone rang and reminded me I needed to plan my responses and questions if she phoned back.  Too late, it was her, I panicked and became a gibbering fool but managed to arrange a meeting for next Wednesday.  The conversation was quite appalling but I guess she did agree so I’m happy but worried about the meeting.
