Monday 8th June
Rudely awakened at 8.30 by my alarm clock, I struggled out of bed, ate and showered and went to college for my 10.30 lecture but, but 10.35 no one had turned up so I left and did e-mailing for an hour or so before meeting Tera and Daisuke for lunch.  After that I went into town with Rich and bought my train ticket to Narita for my homeward trip.  It seems real now.  I bought a chopstick set so I can do the Japanese thing at home.  Rich and I sat in the park for a bit and chatted (mainly about Japanese girls) and then I came back.  Mrs Saiki was late and, thus, so was dinner.  Balàzs phoned and we will go out on Wednesday since I am not teaching and I haven’t seen him for weeks.  I walked the obnoxious sociopathic hound before having an ice cream with the Saikis and coming up to be alone.
