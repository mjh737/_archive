Tuesday 18th November
After my morning lecture I felt ill again so I came home and spent the afternoon reading The Grapes of Wrath which is very good.  I had to go back at 6 o’clock for Nakamura Sensei’s party.  The five of us had a beer first and then went to the party and drunk lots of wine.  Met two v upper class judges whose son (they’re married) is at Tonbridge School.  Met nice wee ladie called Ikumi and managed to cause Nakamura Sensei extreme embarrassment by setting him a kanji which he couldn’t do.  He got his own back later by making the five of us of us do some Japanese Calligraphy and play some obscure Indonesian instruments.  Afterwards, B and I went to the local bar for another beer before going home.  The party was okay but I’m getting a little bored of meeting Japs.  Still, I chatted to Minamikata Sensei and got in his good books.  Made sure it was ok to invite Mum and Dad in March.
