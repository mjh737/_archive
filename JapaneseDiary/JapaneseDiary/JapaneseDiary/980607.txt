Sunday 7th June
I got up bleary eyed at 10.30 and had another solitary breakfast.  I spent most of the day planning various aspects of my homeward journey or reading me book.  I had a nice sushi-based lunch with the Saiki’s.  I’ve decided I will miss Japanese food, taht is providing its not crab’s legs.  Yesterday I was presented with a crab’s leg as big as my arm which I ate, looking like I was enjoying it.  Dinner tonight was okay but, upon asking Mr Saiki what the green algae substance was, I was informed that it was algae that someone had grown in their pond – nice.  I walked Pao and then had an ice cream with the Saiki’s before coming up to study.  All in all an uneventful day, the best part being Mum and Dad’s phone call, especially since I hadn’t spoken to them for a month before that.
