Monday 17th November
I sat around all day doing nothing much, studying and reading Hamlet again when I suddenly realised, at 2 o’clock that I had to meet my tutor, Tera, at 2.30.  I made it despite the hideous rain and we played pool for an hour.  My lecture at 4.20 was okay, Law in Japanese.  Afterwards, I e-mailed Geoff and Sam before, once more, tackling the elements.  Received Jap book and advent calendar from Dad but no Marmite.  Pleased but at the same time disappointed.  Then Mrs Saiki opened a parcel from her son in Tokyo which contained, yes, 4 jars of Marmite – I learnt the word for paradise tonight.  Then, when Mr Saiki came back, he had bought me a brand new, thick-pile, very large rug for my room.  Today was a good day for getting stuff but I have, however, run out of stamps.
