Wednesday 18th February
Wrote back to Mat whilst fighting off my hangover.  Spent about 3 hours getting ready because I couldn’t do anything very quickly.  Cycled to my students’ house.  I’m teaching a 6 year old from 3-4pm and then an 11 year old and his friend from 4pm-6pm.  From this week I am teaching the next door neighbours from 7-8pm.  All at ¥2000 (£10) an hour which is a tidy £40 a week.  It’s very hard work but quite rewarding.  When I got back I asked Mrs Saiki for my driving license back which I left on the table last week.  She reckoned she’d never seen it and 10 minutes of frantic searching followed before it was finally located.
