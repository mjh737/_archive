Wednesday 3rd December
 B was supposed to ring at eleven to arrange to meet up with his Chinese girl friend so I got up at 8 o’clock in order to get some study in beforehand.  I waited downstairs from 10.45-11.15 but he didn’t ring.  Typical – but he rang at 6.30 to tell me he had a great time.  So I spent all day alone studying and reading but I did go out in the freezing cold to buy some gloves and shampoo cos Jap shampoo gives me dandruff (so I bought western stuff).  Got parcel from mum with crossword book in it and cd of Perfect Day Children in Need thing.  Did a couple of crosswords to relieve the boredom and listened to the cd about 20 times so I’m as bored of that as I am with all my other cds.  However, I’m not bored of the Fun Lovin Crims cd which Kate bought me for Xmas and I haven’t opened yet.
