Tuesday 6th January
Got up late and fed the dog (my breakfast) and studied until 4pm when I cycled into Niigata to meet B and Miyako and Miyako’s friend.  I was quite looking forward to this foursome thing but she turned out not to be the most beautiful of girls.  We went to a bar and had a few beers (well, two anyway) before going to McDonalds for dinner.  I was starving so I had the full works.  We then went bowling.  Bowling is one of the few things at which I am really apalling (maybe spelling is another) and I only scored 95.  However, this was better than the other three so I won!  We then went to a games centre and had a few games on Sega Rally.  When we left, there was a blizzard so B, M and I made a dash for Miyako’s apartment and had another beer.  A little later, I cycled home in the arctic conditions and got to bed at around 12.30.
