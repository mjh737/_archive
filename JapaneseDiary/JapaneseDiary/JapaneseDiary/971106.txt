Thursday 6th November
Went to college and used the email.  I got my first ‘e-letter’ (from Sam) which was cool.  I also used the chat function again and spent some time in a cyber room with a bunch of Americans.  We had a weird lecture after lunch.  It was a one off lecture and the woman didn’t realise how long we’d all been studying Japanese and taught us how to say 1,2,3 again!!  Afterwards, I spent an hour with Tera, my tutor.  He’s so boring.  He doesn’t prepare at all.  He just sits there, with his mates and waits for me to speak.  Fortunately I had some grammar questions which took up most of the time.  When I got home I studied for 3 hours before dinner.  Dinner was sukiyaki which was okay.  I paid my rent for October but the Saiki’s tried to stop me.  Still, I managed to persuade them to take it and in return for the £275 Mrs Saiki gave me a few cheap brown envelopes.
