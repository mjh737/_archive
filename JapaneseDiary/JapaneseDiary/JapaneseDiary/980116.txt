Friday 16th January
Studied all day and finished Steinbeck no.5, The Pearl.  Went to Niigata uni station, by foot, to meet Miyako and Balàzs at 5.45.  We went to uni to meet others from our Monday law lectures for a party.  The restaurant was very cheap but we all threw in ¥2500 and got plenty of beer out of it.  Afterwards, we all had a big snow ball fight on the way to karaoke.  This was ¥3000 but we got plenty of singing done and much drinking too.  We did it for hours and then moved on to a Rāmen restaurant for ‘supper’.  By this time it was just me, B, M and Tatsuru.  After the meal, I walked home which seemed to take twice as long as my journey to uni.  On the way home, I bought bread, margarine and cheese so I can have sarnies in my room.  I think Andy has brought a pot of Marmite back from England for me –cool!  I finally crawled drunkenly into bed at around 3.00am.
