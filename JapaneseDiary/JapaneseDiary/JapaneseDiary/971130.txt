Sunday 30th November
Got up at 11 o’clock with a hangover.  Spent the day reading The Grapes of Wrath and studying very little.  Watched TV downstairs for a bit, some sumo wrestling and a standard Japanese quiz show.  Mr Saiki came back with a present for me – a book (second-hand but good condition) with pictures of everything conceivable in it, written in Japanese and English.  So now, I can’t order food in a restaurant but I can name all the parts of a jet foil and dismantle and reassemble a sub-machine gun.
I don’t know what it is about The Grapes of Wrath that I like so much but it’s really cool.  It’s so sad that it’s happy.  The family are just honest ‘folk’ who are really unlucky but the family stick together and help each other.  Highly recommended to everyone.  Congratulations Mr Steinbeck 9/10.
