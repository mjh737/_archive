Wednesday 8th March April (This is just ridiculous!!)
For some reason I am constantly exhausted at the moment.  I set my alarm early but it still seems like the middle of the night when it goes off so I go back to sleep for a few more hours.  Today I finally arose at 11 o’clock, had breakfast and, at last, got round to tidying my room and I feel very good about it now.  At 2.30 I cycled to Usui san’s house to teach, safe in the knowledge that there’ll always be a bit of my heart devoted to it (no, surely that’s wrong).  What I mean is…safe in the knowledge that I didn’t have to teach the neighbours which meant I got home at 7 o’clock with £30 in my back pocket (or, at least, the yen equivalent).  I had a brief dinner of curry for the second night in a row before walking the insane canine beast from the depths of hell and coming up here.  I bought a beer to celebrate only having 84 days to go and, whilst consuming it, I remain absolutely bored to death.  Still, Baby Spice is making April more bearable as she grins at me from her calendar home.
