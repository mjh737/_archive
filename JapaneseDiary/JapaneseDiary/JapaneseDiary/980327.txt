Friday 27th March
Now I’m bored.  It’s 9.14 Friday night and I’m in my room with nothing to do.  Balàzs is in Tokyo in order to meet his sister who comes tomorrow and Rich is boring.  Today started well.  I met Minamikata Sensei who said I can go home 1st July – 96 days to go.  After that I did e-mailing and made enquiries about working for the government after I graduate but to no avail.  We had the usual rāmen lunch and then went back to the library where I played and won cyber-chess with some girl in Pennsylvania called Amy.  We then went home – Rich and I that is, not Amy.  I got some books about Japanese grammar out the library which I’ve been reading.  Had an okay dinner with Mrs Saiki and we chatted a bit but afterwards she went and watched TV in the lounge and left me watching TV alone in the dining room so after half hour I came up here to study but I can’t.  I’m bored.  I think I’ll go over the road and buy a beer.
