Saturday 16th May
Went down for my breakfast of unbuttered toast and then sat around from 9am until 3pm when I went into town to meet Balàzs.  We bought a couple of beers and sat by the river for an hour or so before hitting the diner.  Fortunately Yūmi wasn’t there.  After a meal and a pitcher we decided to go to Magic Shitty and ‘shoot some pool’ which we did and met an English bloke (from Derbyshire) who practically runs the place.  We decided to do an hour of karaoke next but when we got there Balàzs said he couldn’t afford it so we had to go.  Instead we bought a couple more cans of beer and sat in the park chatting for an hour or so.  I caught the last train home and got chatted at by a bunch of students.  Got to bed at about 12.15.
