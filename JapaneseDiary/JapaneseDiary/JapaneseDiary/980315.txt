Sunday 15th March
Did nothing all day today apart from sit in my room and study/write letters.  Balàzs phoned and I shall meet him tomorrow with Rich.  At dinner (the only time I ventured out my room) I mentioned that I would walk Pao but Akihito got up 10 minutes later and walked into the hall with the mutt.  I didn’t mind at all but Mr Saiki followed him out and came back 5 minutes later and slammed the door so hard it scared the hell out of Mrs Saiki and me.  He brought the dog with him.  I know this did not happen for my benefit but have absolutely no idea what caused this argument/dispute.  Anyway, I walked Pao and when I got back, I kept out of their way and came back upstairs to torment myself more with loneliness.  Mum and Dad phoned to say they were safely home.  Goodnight all!
