Tuesday 21st April
I have had a splendid hangover all day today which meant I just stayed in bed.  I went down at about 11 o’clock and had a bowl of cereal which was nice.  Spent most of the afternoon reading my history of the world.  I’ve decided to skip a couple of thousand years and now am reading about the horrific cost of world war one.  I managed to limp to the seven eleven to buy a litre of milk and some bread for lunch.  Mrs Saiki came home for two minutes to tell me that she was going to a conference and seeing that Akihiro and Mr Saiki were not in I was to get my own dinner which was just a case of heating up some curry.  So basically I have had a very lonely (and very welcome under the circumstances) day.  I think I will go to bed shortly to make up for last night’s lack of sleep but first I shall conclude my lesson for the day in Versailles.
