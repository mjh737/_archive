Thursday 16th April
There are 76 days to go and looking back on what I did 76 days ago, it seems to be a long time.  I am not happy.  For dinner I had what I can only imagine Mrs Saiki obtained with a drain plunger, probably including a hair ball, rusty earring and broken contact lens.  I miss chips.  Oh, and other 1970’s cop shows.  Went to uni with Balàzs who I haven’t seen in daylight this year due to the existence of his ‘ball and chain’.  Studied in the library for 5 hours – read loads but feel like I’ve made little progress.  We went for a coffee and sat out in front of the refectory where two groups of first year girls tried to chat us up.  We pretended we couldn’t speak Japanese so they gave up after they had exhausted their set of standard English questions.  Came home after briefly being interviewed by some radio station about whether or not I had a job.  Had dinner, walked Pao and have now slumped into my nightly depression.
