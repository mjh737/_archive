Tuesday 26th May
I got up at 11.30 after finally having a good night’s sleep.  During breakfast Mrs Saiki came back and sarcastically pointed out that I should not say good morning because it was lunch time.  When I came downstairs for coffee an hour later she was fast asleep on the sofa.  An hour later I left her a note (because she was still asleep) and went to college to e-mail and get a book out of the library.  At home I read a bit before dinner and then walked Pao who, attractively, has been throwing up all day.  I phoned Dom about Tokyo on Friday but he has gone away for a few days so, in a panic, I phoned Rich who phoned Nia to find out what’s going on.  Apparently he’s got ‘manky teeth and has gone to Tokyo’ but the weekend is still on.  Now I shall reply to Katy’s letter which I received earlier today.
