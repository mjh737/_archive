Wednesday 5th November
I only had one lecture today (at 12.50) so I stayed in bed until 9am.  Wrote a letter to Mum and Dad and read Trainspotting.  It really is a fantastic book.  After the lecture I made a very successful trip to the post office – a fluent conversation with the bod in the shop.  I hate that word – ‘bod’ – it just reminds me of crap 70’s children’s programmes like Fingermouse and, well, Bod.  Afterwards, Balàzs and I went for a walk around and discovered a good bookshop but were unable to read 95% of the titles so no purchases were made.  When I got home I studied yet again and prepared for tomorrow’s kanji test.  I had a long conversation with Mrs Saiki about international marriages but it was hard going today.  Some days, conversation is easy but on other days I don’t understand anything.  This foreign language lark is not so easy.
