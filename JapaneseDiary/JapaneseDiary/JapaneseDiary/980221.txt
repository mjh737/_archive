Saturday 21st February
Got up at 10.30 and did nothing all day.  I didn’t even study or read.  I just kind of sat and looked at stuff.  However, at 5.00, I went out to meet Balàzs because today’s the day of the Niigata Foreigners party.  We went to the rock’n’roll diner and had dinner and a few beers to get into the mood.  Some Portuguese Australian with a Japanese wife (who wasn’t there) talked at us for half an hour – apparently he lives in London just to confuse his nationality even further.  Also we chatted to George, the Kenyan Olympic boxer.  He’s a big, frightening black man who could quite easily be mistaken for a tank.  Balàzs and I headed off to the party at about 10 o’clock and had to have a few games of Sega Rally on the way.  The party was actually and surprisingly good.  A tiny room full of Westerners including an English girl from London who I chatted to for a bit.  We also got chatted up by some Japanese girls and I failed to chat up this beautiful Brazilian girl before leaving at 3.00 and walking home which took 1½ hours.
