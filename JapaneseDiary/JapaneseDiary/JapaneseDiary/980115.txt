Thursday 15th January
Went into town to meet da boys who went to a special ceremony for people who are twenty.  It’s so important that the whole country takes the day off.  We went to a coffee shop and had average rolls for lunch.  Then B went home but Rich and I went to the cinema to see Peacemaker with George Clooney and Nicole Kidman.  It was very good but me and Rich were right at the front, literally one yard from the screen.  Half way through the film, due to the heavy snow, the roof started leaking on my head which was not nice.  UI had to lean to the left and wear my coat.  Afterwards we hiked back towards the station in the snow drifts and popped into a stationary shop to buy some kanji cards et cetera, and then McDonalds for a small feast.  After a couple of goes on Victory Lap, we went home where I had loads of sushi and horrible egg stuff before walking the dog and coming up here to finish Cannery Row which was only good, not brilliant. (Saw Miho in town with boyfriend!!)
