Thursday 25th June 
I did another practice pack today and, having left a lot of things out, I finally have a manageable suitcase albeit 10kg overweight.  I met Balazs in Bandai at 5pm.  Our big leaving party which we planned had dwindled down to just the two of us.  Richard joined us later though and so did Tatsuru.  Afterwards, since Miki (the other diner waitress I like) had gone home, Tatsuru and I decided to go to Furumachi on a girl-hunt and off we went.  We met a couple of girls (I think) in a print club booth but then went to meet Tatsuru’s Dad who was absolutely ratted and gave Tatsuru fifty quid (not actually that much in Japan).  We then met Meiko and went to a bar before phoning the above mentioned print club ‘girls’ and meeting them for food and beer.  Taturu had somehow phoned them and convinced them that they should treat us – and they did.  After an hour or so we all left.  What we should have done in order to complete our mission is stay in Furumachi but Tatsuru decided to phone Haru (a friend from last year) and ask him to pick us up.  He did and we dropped Meiko off at home before going to Kobari beach (the place to meet girls I was informed – I was a little dubious since it was Thursday and raining).  We chatted to a couple of girls and used the fact that I am foreign to maintain their interest.  However, our interest waned and we decided to back to Furumachy (why didn’t we think of that before?) but by this time the place was dead so we went for a ramen breakfast before driving home.  I got into bed at 5am and actually had a good nights (morning’s) sleep for a change.
