27th June 
Woke up at 1.30pm at Andy’s and staggered home.  I came straight upstairs to sleep more but couldn’t.  I just lay on my bed groaning.  However, my hangover just got worse and worse.  At three o’clock Mrs Saiki invited me down for coffee and we had a pleasant chat for an hour or so, mainly about drinking.  We talked about our experiences involving beer.  Mr Saiki’s only words to me were to tell me, fairly aggressively that I was a piss-head.  I don’t like the man at all and am shocked that he has the audacity to call me this bearing in mind the fact that he only bothers to speak to me once a month.  At 6pm I got ready to go out.  At this stage I wanted to curl up in a ball and die because my head hurt so much.  Instead I had to meet Atsuko (the girl who is studying in England that I met in April (?)).  We had dinner with her parents which was nice but hard work due to my state and I couldn’t eat anything despite all the nice food.  At 10.15pm I left, walked home and came straight up to bed.
