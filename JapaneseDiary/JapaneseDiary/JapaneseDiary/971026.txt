Sunday 26th October
This morning I got up early and went to Niigata with Mr Saiki.  We went to the furniture recycling centre (?!) and then the market followed by a trip to a (mainly children’s) TV studio where I got this (sticker) and others.  At 11.45 he remembered I had to get back to meet R & B and the girls we met on Friday.  I just made it to our 12.00 meeting and we all went into Niigata and had a good day in Sega world.  They are the best looking girls we’ve been out with here.  This picture was taken in a print club machine which is basically a funky passport machine which produces a sheet of 16 photos like these which Jappos find really cool and collect in books, spending approx half of their annual income in the machines.  The girl next to me is Sanpo and she is really ‘cute’ (as the Japanese would say).  Hopefully there will be further developments on that front (or back.  Beggars can’t be choosers).  I’ll leave you now with a picture of a small rodent with beret.
