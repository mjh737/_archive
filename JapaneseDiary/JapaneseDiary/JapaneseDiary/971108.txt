Saturday 8th November
The phone rang at 7.00 and I sprang out of bed only to be confused by some Jap bloke, i.e. not Richard.  I was still asleep and couldn’t remember what country I was in!! Anyway, Sado is off, we’re going tomorrow so I’ve got to get up at 6 o’clock – nightmare!! I met Balàzs in town at 1.30 and we went to the really cool book shop we went to last weekend.  We had lunch in MacDonalds and had a quick go on Sega Rally.  I phoned Dad at 7.00 GMT and woke him up to say phone me tonight but when I got home my host family said they were going to take me back into town so I had to phone back and say phone me on Sunday night at 10 o’clock, i.e. 7am Monday here!  Then I found out Sado was cancelled.  Anyway, Mr S took me to town and bought me a £4 jar of imported English jam and a £60 Jap dictionary as an early Christmas pressie!!!
