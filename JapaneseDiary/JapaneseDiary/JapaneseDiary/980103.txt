Saturday 3rd January
Woke up at 11 o’clock with an average hangover and was disappointed to find that the rain hadn’t washed my puke away so I had to do it manually – how unpleasant.  I snuck downstairs but the Saiki’s were there having breakfast so I had to pretend I was a model of virtue especially seeing as they gave me £25 as a New Year present and another £25 from Mrs Saiki’s mother.  Cycled to Balàzs’ at 4 o’clock and then on to Next 21 to meet his friend from China (a bird) called Lee, or Li or something.  We waited around for ages for her to do stuff and then we had a cheap meal before trying to find another bar.  We failed in our quest so did karaoke for an hour Japanese style, not a sex bar in any way, shape or form.  There were about 10 people there (only two Japs- mostly UK, America).  At the end of the evening the bar man overcharged me ¥2000 and threatened to call the police.  I was furious.
