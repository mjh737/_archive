Monday 19th January
Couldn’t sleep last night but got up at 9.00 after about 4 hours and went for breakfast.  Had a shower and stuff, did a few kanji before cycling in the snow, with wet boots to Balàzs.  It was misery.  We went into town with Rich and I bought a hat and scarf in Daiei where we also had lunch.  We went to Isetjan where I bought a good Mah Jong set which I took back to Balàzs to open but the set was different to ones I’d seen in England, so I took it back because I thought they’d made a mistake.  Fortunately, I checked the other sets which were the same so I’m going to have to get hold of a Mah Jong manual to explain the difference.  I walked Pao and came upstairs and at 10.30, Miho rang (!!?*!!?) which confused and threw me but she was very pleasant and we chatted for 15 mins in Japanese.  I’m going to meet her tomorrow.
