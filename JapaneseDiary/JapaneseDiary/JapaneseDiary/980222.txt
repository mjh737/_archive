Saturday 22nd February
Woke up late – 1.00pm – due to last night.  Had a bit of a hangover but fought it off.  Had a couple of sliced of toast for breakfast and then didn’t eat again until dinner at 7.30pm.  In fact, I didn’t do anything until dinner at 7.30pm.  I just ‘sat’ again like yesterday.  I read a little and studied a little but mostly ‘sat’. I watched the Tokugawa history drama and then walked Pao.  Since then I have studied a bit more but generally it’s been a very dull day.  Still, only 129 dull days to go and I can come home.  Richard phoned and, in March, we may go for a two week sight-seeing tour and do camping and stuff so there’s fourteen days which may be less boring.
