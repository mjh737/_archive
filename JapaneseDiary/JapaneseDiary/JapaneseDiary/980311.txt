Wednesday 11th March
Today was another one of those days when Mr Saiki takes me out knowing that I have to get back at a certain time.  Dad had to wake me this morning cos I wasn’t up at 9.30.  I had actually been awake over an hour and for some reason just laid there singing the Charlatans song ‘Can’t get out of bed’.  I can’t think why.  Mr Saiki rushed us off straight after brekkie to Tahiko shrine where I went a few months ago with Richard’s host family.  It was averagely interesting but I think the olds enjoyed it.  We then had a quick look around some Japanese kind of country park or garden situation which was boring.  We headed back to Niigata and at times could see Sadō Island.  Even though it is the same distance away as France from England, it could be clearly seen since its snow–covered mountains, which are at least as high as Snowdon, were glistening in the sun.  We got back home at about 2.45 and had to rush straight out again in true Saiki fashion to get to Mrs Usui’s house for 3.00.  I spent the next 3 hours trying to educate but ended up just playing with the three brats.  We had a laugh but I was constantly petrified that they were going to smash the place up before Mum and Dad and Mrs Usui returned from their excursion to wherever they went.  When they got back Mrs Usui insisted on paying me £30 for playing Lego all afternoon and I reluctantly accepted it – well, apparently so anyway!  We had Jappo food with Mrs Usui before launching into the small talk again which cued my release into Legoland.  She gave us a lift back whereupon we went straight to bed.
