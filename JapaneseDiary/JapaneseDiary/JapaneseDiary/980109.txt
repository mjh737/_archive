Friday 9th January
Met Rich at 2pm and we went to uni and did e-mail etc before the lecture at 4.30.  After the lecture R and Miyako and B and I went to the western food place just down from the uni.  The food is quite good and food and beer is cheap.  You don’t get to use chopsticks, but these strange metal implements, one with prongs, the other is a kind of blade.  Richard went home early as usual and M, B and I just sat around chatting for a few hours before rushing off to catch the last train.  However, when we got to the station we were stopped by the police for looking Western.  They were quite nice and after seeing our Gaijan cards, just chatted to us about uni and stuff.  After that I decided to walk home which took about an hour cos I stopped off for food on the way.
