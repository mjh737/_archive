Monday 20th April
Went to Uchino to pay a couple of bills and then went to uni to meet Balàzs at 2pm.  The purpose of our meeting was not study but rather an all day bender starting with a trip to the beach.  We spent a couple of hours there and it was lovely – sun, sea, sand and, unfortunately a Hungarian.  However we played in the sand like a couple of kids and then caught the train into town and went to the diner for dinner.  The beautiful girl was there (see entry 6th Feb) and I loved her a lot.  We stayed there for hours and then bought some more beer and went and sat in a little park.  Once the break dancers had gone a group of skateboarders turned up and we watched, amused.  At 3.00am we went home but I only got as far as Hakusan station where I slept on a bench for an hour before completing the last hour of my journey – got to bed at about 5.30am
