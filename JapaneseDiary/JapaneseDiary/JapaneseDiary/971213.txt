Saturday 13th December
Woke up at 9 o’clock and after spending an hour getting ready to meet Miho at uni.  We drove along to ‘seaside line’ and I took her to the cave I went to with Mrs Saiki.  After that we went for a little drive and then home via the video shop.  We watched Transpotting but she fell asleep, thinking it was pretty strange.  Then we watched 101 Dalmatians which was a bit girly but okay.  After that we ordered pizza and I went out to get wine of which Miho drank one bottle – I was dead impressed.  We ate and drank and chatted and it was really cool.  I stayed the night since we both fell asleep in front of the TV – the film Aliens 2 was on which I tried to watch but couldn’t keep my eyes open.  I think I like the girl, perhaps we will become a couple but at this stage it’s hard to tell.  It would be nice though, I think.  (Think again – ed 24/03/98)
