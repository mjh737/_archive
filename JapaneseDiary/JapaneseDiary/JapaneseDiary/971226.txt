Boxing Day
(or ‘Friday’ as it’s called in Japan)
Cycled to Uchino to pay my health insurance and went to the shops on the way back.  Cycled into Niigata to meet Balàzs and Miyako at 3pm.  We went to Joypolis and, after playing driving games for an hour, watched the sun set over the Shiano river – very beautiful – I’m getting old!  Then we went for a beer in a café but decided to move on to the American Diner for the evening.  There, I had fajitas and ½ price beer cos it was happy hour.  The DJ was a sexy-voiced Japanese girl and she invited requests so I wrote down about twenty and was happy that she played five and smiled at me everytime.  We stayed there until twelve and consumed our cheap beer but it was still quite an expensive evening.  We walked back to Miyako’s and Balàzs and she went inside and left me to cycle home, cold, lonely and depressed.  At least Christmas is over now.
