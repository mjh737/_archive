Thursday 22nd January
Woke up for college at 7.00 but couldn’t face it so went back to sleep for an hour and a half before walking to meet Rich after his lecture.  Balàzs wasn’t there and the computers were out of action so we sat in the refectory and played chess until lunch time when B turned up.  The three of us went to the Rāmen place for lunch and then played Mah Jong for an hour.  Rich and I got the train into town to do a bit of shopping and I bought a beginners Mah Jong book.  Came home and packed ALL my stuff for the ski trip tomorrow.  I’ve dreamt about skiing since I was a little kid and now, despite the fact that I’m going tomorrow, am not really excited about it at all!  I walked Pao and had a bath before finishing ‘The Red Pony’ by John Steinbeck and going to bed early.
