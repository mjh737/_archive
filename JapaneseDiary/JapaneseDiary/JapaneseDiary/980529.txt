Friday 29th May
Left home at 8am and got the train in to meet Rich.  Caught the bus at 9am and finally got to Ikebukuro at about 2pm.  Another 30 minutes of travelling and we got to some deserted station where Rich had an interview for an hour.  It took us about 50 minutes to find the place and then there was no time for lunch so we just had a couple of sandwiches from Lawsons.  While Rich was at his interview I caught the train to Shinjuku for half an hour before coming back to find Rich waiting for me.  His interview was shorter than he expected.  We caught the train to Ebisu (where I saw Misa et al last week) and waited for our Cardiff friends.  It was good to see them (Tamsin, Cath, Nia along with an English bloke they had met).  
We had a quick beer in the English pub I went to last week before going back to the station to meet a couple more people.  We had to wait an hour and a half but whilst doing so we bought beer and, another amazing Tokyo coincidence (see 13th March), we bumped into Shimizu Sensei from Cardiff.  So all of us went to a restaurant for a tiny meal and another beer before ditching the teacher and heading off to Roppongi where the night was really to begin.  We tried to do karaoke but it was far too expensive so we went to some bar where we kind of split up.  My party went on to another place where they throw you out if you’re not drinking.  I got fed up and waited outside but by this time it was...
