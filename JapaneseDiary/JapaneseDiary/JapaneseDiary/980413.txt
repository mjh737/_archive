Monday 13th April
Couldn’t get up (from the floor) to meet Rich at uni at 10 and he phoned me to find out where I was.  I explained that I was feeling a little under the weather and agreed to meet him on the train at 2.00 instead.  My host mother stuffed me full of food to the extent that I almost threw it back up in front of her and then she gave me some anti-hangover medicine which was, for want of an obscenity, disgusting.  I met Rich and we checked out the new Joyopolis which was rubbish and will be so until the real opening on Saturday.  We had a look around a few shops and then sat down and watched the world going by for an hour.  Came home and had dinner with just Mrs Saiki since Akihiro has started night school.  Walked Pao and came up here to suffer in peace.
