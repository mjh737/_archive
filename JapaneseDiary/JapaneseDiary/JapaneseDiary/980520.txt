Wednesday 20th May
All morning I sorted stuff out.  I realised I had a financial crisis on my hand.  The situation is dire and I therefore have a bad case of ‘dire arrears’ – ha ha ha.  I wrote a couple of letters, studied a bit and then got ready to go to the Usui’s to teach.  Just as I was leaving I got a letter from Hitomi in England which took a good 10 minutes to translate/read.  I was late getting to my students house and when I did Mrs Usui had to go out leaving me in control of the three brats which was horrendously difficult.  I had dinner and a beer there before moving on to the Takizawa’s for a lesson with them and their friends.  Cycled home, walked Pao and here I am, absolutely exhausted and ready for bed.  Goodnight.
