Wednesday 15th October
Right that does it!  No more Japanese.  I can’t do it anymore.  I’m too tired and my head hurts.  From now on I will utter not a word of the Devil’s tongue.  I can DO English and I will do only English.  Today has been a stressful day of translation and conversation, almost all of which required a dictionary.  I finished my presentation for next week.  Saiko checked it but Mrs Saiki reckons it’s all wrong.  At least one good thing happened.  Rich has invited me to a party at his house and Asami will be there.  Unfortunately, a) she doesn’t speak English and b) Rich has been telling his host Mum that I’m practically in love with her.  This is a potentially humiliating situation which I shall endeavour to conquer with a mutually convenient arrangement for both myself and the young Japanese lady in question.
