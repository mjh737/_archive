Monday 15th June
Got up at 8.30 and went to my first lecture for a month.  Afterwards I met Tera and we chatted fairly well for a bit.  We talked about our different music tastes and promised to buy each other a cd and give them to each other next Monday.  Went to Bandai with Rich, bought Tera’s cd and a soup bowl for me to drink Muso from in England.  Talking of which, today was the first England match (Tunisia) so me, Andy and Rachel painted our faces, got the beers in and sung and cheered our national team to a 2-0 victory.  We became very drunk.  Andy threw up and went to bed.  I have no recollection after that point but remember Rachel making me up a bed of chairs because I couldn’t make it to their room where I could have had a real bed.
