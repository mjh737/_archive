Tuesday 2nd December
It’s winter – no, really winter – I cycled home from college in the dark.  It was thundering, really windy and very cold.  I was wearing my full waterproofs, the only parts of my body exposed were my face and hands but it was agony.  The rain, hail, sleet, snow, whatever cut into my flesh and hurt so I tried cycling with my hands up my sleeves but that just pulled my coat down, exposing my face more.  It took ages.  Mrs Saiki says it’s not really going to get wintery for another month!!  Kanji test was great today – I must’ve got 100%.  Did e-mailing and e-chatting for about 4 hours – great fun chatting to people all round the world.  Spent 3 hours chatting with the Saikis but, although my Japanese started out pretty good, I became tired and frustrated.  I’ve got my heater on now and the room temperature is 16˚c which feels quite warm but everything, including this diary, is freezing to the touch.  This may well be my last entry due to hypothermia.
