Saturday 24th January
Today was a good day.  I had a bit of a hangover but was looking forward to proper skiing too much.  We got to the slope at about 10 o’clock and had an hour and a half of lessons but A & R & B & I decided to go down the beginners slope which I found easy.  So after lunch I went down the intermediate one with Rich who has been a few times before and after 3 of those I went on the ridiculously dangerous expert one.  I couldn’t do it without falling over but it was great fun and really fast.  I’ve got the ski bug now and must go again.  Back at the hotel we had another little sleep before dinner and another party.  We didn’t drink so much or mingle again but played a good few drinking games.  Much fun was had by all.  Went back to the room and chatted and drank whiskey until late.
