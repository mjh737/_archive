Saturday 29th November
Today the weather was absolutely hideous – windy, cold and rainy but, having donned my wet weather gear I cycled to Balàzs’s at about 5ish.  Went out with his host brother, Hisaei, to a new building which had a trendy kind of wine bar on the ground floor.  Saw an Irish band there and met a Canadian called Kelly who was a man and a tax auditor.  So we left him and went to Geos language school to meet Martin-the-Canadian and his American friend Cynthia and a Jap girl.  Went to a restaurant and had many many beers and little food.  At 2.00 I cycled home drunk, in the rain, and at one stage managed to crash at full speed into a tree and fall off, quite uncomfortably into a puddle.  Fortunately my new wet weather gear worked wonders.
