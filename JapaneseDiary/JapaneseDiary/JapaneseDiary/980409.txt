Thursday 9th March April (This must be some sort of joke!!)
The Japanese have an annoying habit.  Actually, they have several but one was especially noticed today.  They never say ‘yesterday’ or ‘the day after tomorrow’ or ‘a week Tuesday’ even though such words exist.  No, instead they say the date.  For example, a few weeks ago Mrs Saiki visited her friend who has a daughter studying in England and she is home at the moment so Mrs Saiki invited her round.  So at breakfast this morning Mrs Saiki told me they’re coming round on the 10th and it took most of the day to successfully translate that as ‘tomorrow’.  So, I am quite looking forward to it but she’s bound to be like the last one (just after Christmas).  Today, apart from studying, I did 500 sit-ups but for some reason am in no pain whatsoever.  I must be doing them wrong.  Had dinner, walked Pao and now I am about to study again.
