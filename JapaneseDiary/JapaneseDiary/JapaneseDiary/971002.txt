Thursday 2nd October 1997 7.00 a.m.
I woke up at 5:30 a.m. and can’t go back to sleep, jetlag and all.  I’ve just been laying here for the last hour and a half feeling sorry for myself.  I want to do something to keep myself busy but I’ve just got to hang around until I go to uni at 1:00 p.m.  Anyway, I’ll leave this until I can be more constructive.  ‘365 and I wake up’ ! (KT – Remember?).  Had a meeting – met Saiko and Hiromi and two Bristol students, Rachel and Andy (I think!).  Andy knows Kirsty from Cardiff – small world.  Went to City Hall to get Gaijin Card and cycled around Kobari.  Spent two hours putting a map together with Saiki-san but the most important page was missed out.  There are about 25 pages, all bigger than A4, all glued together, far too big to be practical but it’s the thought that counts.  Went to bed and completed a Japanese language evaluation questionnaire and read a little ‘Trainspotting’ – it’s very good so far – thanks Kate.
