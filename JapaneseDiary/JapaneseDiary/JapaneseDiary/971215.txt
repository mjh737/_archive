Monday 15th December
Didn’t even wake up until 1.00.  Stumbled around trying to get ready with a dreadful hangover.  Had a painful trip to college to meet Tera at 2.30.  Had a boring chat for an hour then went to the library to do some e-mailing.  While I was there, Yō, my high school girl tapped me on the shoulder and, since I couldn’t face my lecture with such a hideous hangover, I took her to the refectory where I helped her with her English homework and she taught me a few Japanese young persons slang kind of thing.  She looked very good but I think perhaps she is a trifle young.  Cycled home and told host Mother that I lost my lock and that I will replace it.  She was none too impressed.  Escaped early and came upstairs to study for tomorrow’s kanji quiz.
