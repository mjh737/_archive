Thursday 30th April
Today was busy.  I met Rich at 10 o’clock and we went to uni to e-mail.  At 12 o’clock we called for Andy and together went down to the football pitch and met up with a load of Japs and Chinks for a game.  We played for an hour and I really enjoyed it.  We had a little lunch outside the refectory before meeting up with Balàzs.  B & I went into town and got our hair cut.  I asked for a Brad Pitt haircut and pretty much got it.  I think Balàzs must have asked for a chimpanzee haircut because it is really quite short.  I raced home, had a shower and then went back to uni to meet Andy and Rachel at 7 o’clock.  We drove to Mick’s house.  Mick is the pro Sinn Fein N. Irish bloke and today is his birthday.  I don’t really like him but since it was a night out I had to go.  We went into Furumachi to an Italian restaurant in an area I had never seen before.  It was quite a nice restaurant despite the cockroach which scaled the wall during the meal.  There were 12 people there and me and Rachel spent most of our time chatting to a couple of American girls.  At the other end of the table was a beautiful German girl but I didn’t get a chance to speak to her.  We moved on to a small bar afterwards, run by this cool little Jap bloke.  Basically around the whole bar was his CD collection which included every band I like and everything they ever recorded.  I chatted to him for ages and we swapped ideas but I got more from him than he did me.  I think he may even have known more than Geoff.  Went back to Rachel’s had another beer then walked home.  Got to bed at 4am.
