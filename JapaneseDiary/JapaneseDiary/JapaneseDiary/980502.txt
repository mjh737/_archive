Saturday 2nd May
I woke up at 3am and had hay fever misery for an hour so at 4am I got up and read my new book.  I snuck downstairs and had coffee a few times before Mrs Saiki got up 7 hours later.  Today was just stupidly hot and I couldn’t go out.  I just laid on my bed all day reading and sweating profusely.  After dinner it was still horrible and I walked Pao with only a t-shirt but still came back sweaty.  Apparently summer is even hotter but first we have to go through the June/July rainy season which I imagine to be wet but humid.  I bought a beer to cool myself down and came straight back up here to drink it and read my book ‘Rule of Law’ by Dexter Dias which is okay but appallingly written.
