Thursday 4th June
I was bored all day today.  Too lethargic to study or tidy my room.  I just sat in a room full of my own farts and did nothing but look forward to my brief date with Yūmi at 10.pm tonight, trying to figure out what we could do other than just walk home.  Anyway at 4pm on the dot she phoned to cancel, saying she had urgent business.  At 4.05pm I phoned Rachel to arrange an alternative evening drowning my sorrows.  We agreed to meet at Mashies at 6.30pm.  We drank and chatted until 11.30pm when Andy turned up.  The three of us walked back to International House, Rachel falling instantly asleep but Andy and I say on the balcony and chatted for an hour or so before I faced the hours walk home which, I seem to remember, I quite enjoyed for some unknown and incomprehensible reason.
