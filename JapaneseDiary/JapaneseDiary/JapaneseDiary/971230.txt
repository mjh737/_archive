Tuesday 30th December
Got a lift home at 9.30 from my host Aunt who seems quite nice.  Got ready for my ‘date’ (used very loosely) with the Illinois uni girl, Yuki.  We went into Bandai and the usual joypolis.  We played a few games and had a coffee.  She asked me if I had a girlfriend.  I said ‘yes’ (a lie).  I asked her if she had a boyfriend and she said she never had one.  I said ‘I can’t believe that’ (another lie.  It’s easy to believe.  She’s pig ugly and has the social skills of a dead turkey).  Still, out of kindness I spent the day with her because she enjoys my company and thinks I’m ‘handsome’.  We went to a few new and interesting shops including a bargain cd stall where I purchased two for ten pounds.  We had coffee and then came home.  She asked me to go out with her and her friend on Sunday and, as another gesture of pity, I agreed.  Hopefully, she will appear as an angel in comparison to Yuki but we’ll have to see.  If you, the reader, are female, you will be interested to learn that this is exactly how men always talk about women.
