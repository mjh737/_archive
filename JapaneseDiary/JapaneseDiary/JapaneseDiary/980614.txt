Sunday 14th June
I swear my suitcases are anti-‘tardis’es – they seem to be much smaller on the inside than on the outside.  I realised this because today I did a trial pack and, when both suitacases were full, discovered I had not yet put my clothes in.  However the main problem is that they are already 10kg overweight so I’m going to have to ditch about 87 pairs of pants to get it down to an acceptable figure.  Apart from that, I spent the whole day being quite energetic and getting stuff done.  After dinner I went to uni to watch the Argentina vs Japan match.  It was one of those matches where I wanted both teams to lose.  Japan has entered the tournament with the same bravado and national confidence that it did the Pacific war and has not considered a defeat.  It is a shame that Croatia and Jamaica will also beat them because England will not be able to knock them out in the next round.  Walked home (75 minutes) and got to bed at 3am.
