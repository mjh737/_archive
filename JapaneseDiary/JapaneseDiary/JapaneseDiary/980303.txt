Tuesday 3rd March
Woke up to a glorious morning at 9.30.  Had breakfast and saw to my ablutions.  Came back up and pottered around reading, studying and cleaning until 2pm when I decided I would cycle to the cheap ‘super value’/ kester-type shop where I purchased some A1 drawing paper so I can embark on larger scale art projects.  I spent the rest of the afternoon drawing and colouring in a huge map of the world.  Why? I don’t know but it was kind of interested relating places I’m raeding about in my world history book to their actual geographical locations and, besides, it wasted another four hours of Japan time – 120 days and I wake up.  Mrs Saiki asked me why I wasn’t at the University Foreign Students Party and I replied with, ‘What party?’ So that was nice.  She then proceeded to interfere with my plans for next week when my parents come.  Once again I decided that I wanted to be alone and made my excuses and came back up here to stare at the carpet.
