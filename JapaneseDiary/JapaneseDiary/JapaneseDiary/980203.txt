Tuesday 3rd February
My alarm went of at 7.00 but I couldn’t bring myself to get out of my warm, comfortable bed so I laid there for another 3 hours before having toast for breakfast.  Mrs Saiki came back at 11 o’clock and invited me down for tea and cake and a chat which was nice.  I tried to leave it until the last minute before going to college so I could be late but she insisted on giving me a lift which was quite sweet.  She asked me if I have a cd walkman so I think she plans to get me one for my birthday.  In the car I mentioned my lack of camera a few times just in case she can’t get a walkman!!  After the lecture I went into town for a bit with the boys and had McDons for lunch.  Balàzs and I walked miles to get our hair cut but the place was closed so I’m going to do it tomorrow.  Came home and had dinner and, after Pao threw up under the table, I came upstairs to study and write today’s diary entry.
