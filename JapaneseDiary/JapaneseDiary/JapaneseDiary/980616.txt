Tuesday 16th June
Today is Tuesday.  In two weeks time I will be on my way home.  It’s quite a scary prospect really.  There are many things here which have become part of my life and which I will miss when I get back to England.  Today I woke up alone in the International House community room.  I tidied up and then became aware of my need for a fry up.  Andy and I went shopping for the ingredients and a veritable feast was had by all.  I went home and sat around mostly reading for the rest of today.  Tonight there is no good football so I can have a well-deserved night off.  I walked Paoser and came upstairs the moment I got back.  I need to study but I feel so exhausted I shall probably read in ebd for an hour before sleeping.
