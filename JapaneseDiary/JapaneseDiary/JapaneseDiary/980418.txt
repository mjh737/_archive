Saturday 18th April
I went into town to meet Balàzs at 3.30pm and we bought a couple of beers and went and sat by the river for a couple of hours before meeting up with Rich and Miyako in order to go to a party with Daisuke (Tera’s friend) who had invited us last week.  It was actually quite a good cheese and wine ‘do’ and we spoke to loads of people in the 3 hours we were there.  Drank loads of wine and left at 9 o’clock and went to a little Japanese restaurant for snacks and, reluctantly, beer which Balàzs couldn’t drink so Rich, yes Rich, and I drank it.  Balàzs was absolutely wasted – I’ve never seen him this bad before.  We caught the last train home and met Andy who had been to the diner.  Somehow I got home but I’ve really got no recollection as to my route but I think I bought some food on the way.
