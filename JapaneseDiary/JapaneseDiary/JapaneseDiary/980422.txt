Wednesday 22nd April
This morning I read more of my world history book and then spent a couple of hours on my dissertation.  At 2.30 I cycled to see my students.  I think they are all bored of learning now perhaps because of the novelty of meeting a new foreigner has worn off.  The girl, Yoshimi, seems to be making most progress and she could probably become good if I could teach her everyday rather than once a week.  Anyway I’m doing my best and they all get their money’s worth.  At 6pm I cycled home and waited around until 8.00 when Mrs Saiki came home and made me dinner.  Hardly said a word since they said none.  After dinner I walked ‘It’ and went back and sat in the living room but they again said nothing even when I spoke so I came upstairs.  I’ll listen to the radio for a while now in a last ditch attempt to pick up spoken Japanese.
