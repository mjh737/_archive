Monday 8th December
I’ve been here about 70 days now which is quite a lot really.  I must be a quarter of the way through.  As I write this, there is a Christmas song (“And so this is Christmas”) on Niigata FM which is making me a little homesick.  I can just imagine everyone who is of any importance to me – Tun Wells, Cardiff and Tonbridge down the Sussex singing it.  Anyway, back to reality, today I finished Grapes of Wrath, which, although it ends rather abruptly, is one of the best books I’ve ever read.  I felt quite sad leaving the Joad family to their poverty and never being able to know if they were okay in the end or not.  Anyway, back to reality again, went to play pool  with Tera and then had a law lecture which was fun!  Came home and studied a bit, walked dog etc – the usual.
