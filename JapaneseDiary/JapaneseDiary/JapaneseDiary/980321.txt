Saturday 21st March
Hungover and miserable I went down to breakfast at about lunchtime.  Mr Saiki was on his way out and Mrs Saiki already out.  Akihiro was, naturally, asleep in the lounge.  I had the two remaining pieces of bread but, still hungry, walked to the shop and bought some sandwiches and more bread so I could have more toast.  Whilst I ate that, Mrs Saiki came back and gave me loads more food.  I was stuffed.  I went back to bed for a bit and then studied for an hour before dinner.  I was too ill to do anything really.  I think I shall go to bed early, about 11 o’clock, and read but I will get up early too (about 8.30) because I’ve recently read that no matter how much sleep you have, you are generally less tired if you get up at the same time every day.  This explains why I am so tired today.  Last night I went to bed not long before I usually get up.
