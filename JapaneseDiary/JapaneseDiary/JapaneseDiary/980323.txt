Monday 23rd March
It’s turned quite cold recently… What am I doing talking about the weather?  Anyway, I decided to brave it and cycle into town.  For the first 6 hours of my day I had studied and by 2.30 I was a little bored.  I bought a kanji card box and after walking around the usual shops for an hour I bumped into, or rather I was bumped into by, Rich who had also got bored.  We had a coffee and chatted a bit and then, as it started to drizzle,  I cycled home.  Mr Saiki was also out this evening so Akihiro and I had another silent take-away curry and then I walked the slightly more talkative dog.  Now I’m here in my room and I’m ‘all studied out’ so I think I’ll retire in about half an hour (12.00) and besides, I layed awake all last night worrying about what the hell is going on and trying to find the motivation to keep it going on.  I think tomorrow I’ll do a tape for Geoff in the afternoon to express my appreciation for the one he did for me the other day.
