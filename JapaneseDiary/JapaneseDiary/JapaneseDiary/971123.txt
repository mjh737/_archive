Sunday 23rd November
Woke up at Balàzs’s and after a slice of toast, we rushed to the station with severe hangovers.  Back here, we set out with Mrs S on our expedition.  First, we visited a temple which had a billion year old Japanese monk mummified in a box.  We moved on to a weird 3D optical illusion type art museum which was rather peculiar but interesting. After that we went to an aquarium which was quite interesting too.  Balàzs, with his unnatural love of aquatic animals, had a great time.  We had steak for lunch before returning home.  B & I then went out for our first curry in Japan; the restaurant was very nice with a good English-speaking waitress.  We had the full works but no chutneys were forthcoming.  However, the curry was, without a doubt, the hottest I’ve ever eaten.  We returned to chez Saiki and had a few beers before bed.
