Sunday 21st December
Went into Niigata and met Rachel and Richard to have lunch with some people Rachel met once.  We ended up in the rock ‘n’ roll diner.  Everyone was foreign, Jordanian, Lebanese, Mexican etc.  One girl was Russian and she was really quite attractive.  I think I might go to Russia next!  My food didn’t turn up much to my annoyance so I cancelled it and ate half of Rachel’s.  After, Richard and I went for a few games in Joypolis before going home.  Here I sat around for a 2½ hours waiting for Mum and Dad to call and then went to dinner.  Chatted to Mrs Saiki about my dissatisfaction with my personal tutor.  She said he was just ‘meek’.  Perhaps she is right – he really is hard to talk to but he seems to be quite a nice bloke.  Since 9 o’clock I’ve been in my room reading East of Eden but I can’t help feeling a bit bored.  I wonder if I’m going to be lonely this Christmas without you.
