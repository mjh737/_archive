Saturday 7th February
Had a hangover from hell this morning but still managed to get dressed and cycled into Niigata.  I went alone and only because the weather was good.  Two tears ago I used to love winter and hate summer but recently I’ve been longing for sun, t-shirts and shades.  I ended up buying another 300 kanji cards which I spent the rest of the day filling in.  I also bought a CD by an American country rock type band called Son Volt (which Geoff recommended by e-mail) which is good but undoubtedly one of the most depressing things I’ve ever heard.  It’s just what I need.  When I got back from town, Mr Saiki made me spend an hour cleaning my bike but he did help me so it’s fair enough I guess.  After dinner, I walked the repulsive monster dog and watched a bit of tv before studying again.  I think I can read 1000 kanji now which means just one more thousand to go.
