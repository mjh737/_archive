Sunday 8th February
Woke up with a start because I had an all too real nightmare about a plane crash.  The plane had just taken off and the stalled, gliding down very steeply and crashing into the ground in a burst of flames.  Hoping this was not a bad sign about my parents coming, I went downstairs to find Mrs Saiki visibly upset because during the night her friend, his wife and their children were killed in a fire.  Spooky!  I spent most of the day out of the way in my room studying and drawing which was a bit boring.  Mrs Saiki got some great pizza in for lunch and dinner was pretty good too – some kind of thick pastry gyōza, dripping with lard.  I walked the dog and then came back up and decided to sort my life out.  So, I wrote a whole bunch of letters and sorted my paperwork out.  I still have a big list of things to do especially with regard to finding a place to stay in Tokyo in two weeks and what I’m going to do with my parents for a week next month.  There’s nothing really interesting to do here – not holiday stuff anyway.
