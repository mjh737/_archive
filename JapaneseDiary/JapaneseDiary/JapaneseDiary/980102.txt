Friday 2nd January
Got up at 7.30 and went down to make myself a boiled egg.  The only reason I got up this early was to make my own breakfast.  However, Mrs Saiki had made my egg the night before.  It was cold and horrible.  Met Balàzs and his host brother at 12.00 and went to another party at Sumiyo’s house.  There was a Canadian man called Rocky and an Olympic boxer from Kenya called George.  You could tell from his voice and his handshake that he was a boxer.  We got drunk by a bout 2 0’clock and at 6 .00 went to another party at Balàzs’s house and met lots of Hisaei’s friends who were all about 30/31 years old with no fanciable qualities.  We drank a shed load which took about an hour or more.  In the middle of the night (4.00am) I suddenly woke up, ran to the verandah and threw up a healthy amount of the days food before going back to bed.
