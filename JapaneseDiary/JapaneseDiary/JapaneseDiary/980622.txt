22nd June 
What a day!  But let’s start with the night.  I went to bed at 12 o’clock last night but by 1 o’clock I wasn’t asleep so I got up and wrote to Emma and went back to bed for a retry at 2am.  Unfortunately it was hopeless so at 4am I got up and started pottering around.  I went to uni early and sent e-mails to everyone saying I won’t be sending e-mails to anyone.  After that was the Japanese exam which was pretty easy actually.  Then I met Tera who I made an arrangement with last week for us each to buy a CD of our choice for the other.  I gave him my Ł10 D and he gave me 4 totalling over sixty quid.  I was quite shocked and am still looking for a motive. (Oh yeah, it’s because he wants friends in the UK next year).  Anyway, Rich and I and Andy went for our last ramen lunch together and then caught the train into town.  I bought a few presents, well four actually – all the same thing.  I came home and sorted stuff out for an hour or so before Mrs Saiki came home.  Had a nice gyoza dinner and found that the closer I am to going home, the nicer she is becoming.  Unfortunately Mr Saiki is still an antisocial arsehole.   Actually, I even had a conversation with Akihiro today albeit a very short one.  After dinner I caught the train to uni and was there by 9am.  The England/Romania match was not until 4am and I was a little worried about the fact that I hadn’t slept last night and whether I’d stay awake.  The beer didn’t help either but with a great show of willpower I managed to stay awake until the match but was distraught to see us lose to a side who I imagined we’d walk over.  At 6am Rich and I walked back to the station, miserable.  I got to bed at 7am.
