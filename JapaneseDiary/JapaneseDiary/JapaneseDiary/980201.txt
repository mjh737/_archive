Sunday 1st February
Got up early again and had a large Kawase-san breakfast before leaving to ski.  It took a while to find the Jeep cos 2ft of snow had fallen overnight, but eventually we managed to locate it and dig it out.  We went to a nearby slope and they hired ski stuff for me.  Rich & I went straight to the top and for a couple of hours I didn’t fall over at all.  At 1 o’clock, we went down to meet up and I decided to go flat out all the way.  Near the bottom I decided to wait for Rich so I started to stop but some bloke crashed into me at 90º at a combined speed of about 80mph.  I almost broke my jaw and could see nothing but stars for ages.  I only have a swollen, bruised thumb left now.  We had a huge lunch on the way home and arrived back at about 4pm.  I had dinner at 7pm after Mum & Dad phoned.  Then I walked Pao before coming up here to prepare for Tuesday’s lectures.
