Sunday 28th June 
Despite feeling terrible yesterday, today I arose at 8am.  I had breakfast and came back up.  At 10am, Mrs Saiki phoned me and said it was time to go to the antiques fair which she briefly mentioned yesterday.  We went but on the way we went to a Chinese garden which, in spite of the fact that I am only 26, was quite nice.  When we came home she ordered pizza and I ate lots and nearly puked.  I’ve been feeling rough anyway and have decided that yesterday’s illness was a cold and not beer-induced at all.  This afternoon I pottered around a bit before my farewell party with the Saiki’s (Mr Saiki has chosen not to be seen anymore and the tosser was, fortunately, not in).  After the meal I watched TV for a bit and then walked the fetid canine killer beast for the penultimate time.  Had three phone calls today (Tatsutu, Daisuke and Ayoko) and I will be seeing them tomorrow.  SO now I must pack and sleep...goodnight.
