Sunday 12th October
The three of us were supposed to go to a flea market today with the Saikis’ but it was cancelled this morning due to inclement weather conditions.  Instead, Balàzs and Rich came here to be tormented by the dog for an hour or so.  Then we went to the river ‘to play’ followed by a trip to the second-hand book shop where I purchased a manga comic book which is practically impossible to translate due to its ‘colloquiality’.  All afternoon I’ve done nothing apart from chat to the Saikis’ about world peace for so long I am now pro-war.  They told me how unracist they (Japanese) are, oh apart from the Koreans who they hate so vehemently that the lives’ work of Martin Luther King at al seems to be meaningless.  After that I wrote my diary before doing something else which has not yet been decided.
