Tuesday 23rd December
Got up at 8 o’clock and cycled to Niigata to get the last of my Christmas shopping done.  Balàzs whacked me over the back of the head as a gesture of friendship and introduced me to his good friend  Fusako.  I cycled home heavily laden with presents.  I had to meet Balàzs (the same Balàzs) at Kobari station at 1.30 for to go to a party with his host brother.  We did the aforementioned and drank loads since it was free.  We stayed there until I don’t know what time and then moved on to Mashie’s where I met Yō and Tomo who weren’t in very good moods perhaps because their boyfriends were there.  Yō’s boyfriend was one of these pathetic no-ideas-of-his-own wears-his-sunglasses-in-his-hair-at-night fashion victims so I called him a homosexual just to set the record straight.  Perhaps I had drunk too much.
