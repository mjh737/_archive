Monday 24th November
B stayed over here last night and neither of us got up until 11 o’clock.  Mr S took us to a cave.  It was half way up a mountain and seemed to be just a small hole in the ground but when we got inside, it was a low, narrow cave which we had difficulty negotiating with the Saikis’ pathetic torch, but we managed to go really deep & far – it was cool.  After that, we went to a kind of manor house, very nice but only about 80 years old.  Then we went to a big shrine/temple complex and had a look around followed by a trip to this lake where lots of Japanese people go to look at lots of Japanese swans who go  there to look at a lot of Japanese people.  We returned home at about 6 o’clock but it was not until 7.30 that we ate, 8½  hours after my last meal.  I was almost unable to eat due to starvation!  I tried to study for 1½ hours for tomorrow’s kanji test but I’m too tired so I’ll have to get up at 6.30am tomorrow to do it.
