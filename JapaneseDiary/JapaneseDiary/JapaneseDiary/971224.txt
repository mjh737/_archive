Christmas Eve
Got up with a hangover.  Spent the whole day just kind of sitting and staring at stuff.  I was waiting for Miho to call but she didn’t.  At 5.30, Mrs Saiki came home and I told her that I had to go out at 6.30.  She then went out and came back at 6.25 with a KFC bucket for the four of us which I had to sit and eat.  I then dished out my presents with which I think they were well pleased.  Mr and Mrs Saiki said ‘Thank you’ in English and Akihiro said ‘uh’ which I guess must mean ‘thank you’ as well as ‘hello’, ‘goodbye’ and ‘it’s dinner time’.  Went to Ryūgaku Centre party and played guitar and got free beer.  Went to Mashie’s afterwards and played pool for 2 hours.  Drunk and drunk and went to Miho’s.  The lights were on but no-one came to the door.  Went to Rachels and stayed over.
