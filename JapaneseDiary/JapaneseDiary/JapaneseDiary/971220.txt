Saturday 20th December
Got up at 11 o’clock with a bit of a hangover.  Caught the train to Niigata to meet Rich at 1.00pm.  As usual, everyone stared at me.  Basically the whole train is averagely full but all the girls, when they run to get on the train, see me and say ‘here’ to each other and get on so the train is really packed around me!  Usually this would be a good thing but not when you feel hungover and miserable.  Rich and I went to the big department store to buy Christmas presents for our host families but I just ended up buying an electronic translator thing for £20 which is good and a piece of cheddarish cheese which I consumed greedily and immediately upon leaving the shop.  Came home and had dinner.  I’ve just walked Pao and while I was out I was peering into all the candle-lit, steamed up restaurants with their Christmas glow like I was a little pauper outside the houses of the rich on Christmas Day.  I miss home.
