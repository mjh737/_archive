Wednesday 1st October 1997
Well I’m here now and it’s finally sunk in – I’m here for a year, not there.  I know I’ll only feel miserable for a week or two about leaving things behind so I’ll just get on and try to enjoy it.
The flight left on time(ish) and was very long, the boredom not being broken at all by the three crap films.  Four hours of waiting and train travelling followed this and only cost 65 quid.  The Saiki’s are very nice although conversation is very hard; Japanese food may take a little longer to appreciate.  Must go to bed now, it’s nearly nine o’clock; I’ve been up about 50 hours.  Spent 30 mins with Saiki Jnr after this – quite good.
