Sunday 29th March
Today was a nothing day again.  I didn’t even leave the house.  I got up as usual at 8.00 and studied until the Saikis got up at midday when I went down for lunch.  All afternoon I sat around on my own waiting to go back to England.  I read quite a lot of ‘For Whom the Bell Tolls’ but can think of nothing else constructive to report.  Dinner was kind of meat patty things, two thereof, with three bowls of green vegetables (I think) which I just swallowed.  Mum and Dad phoned and it was nice to chat to them.  After that I chatted to Mrs Saiki for a while about what the hell I want to do with my life but I really have little idea.  I mentioned the everlasting desire to be a guitarist and she suggested forming a band with the gimp (Akihiro) – yeah right.  I watched a little, well, medium-sized, TV for half hour before coming up.  Balàzs phoned to confirm tomorrow’s trip out with his sister and I am looking forward to it very much.
