Wednesday 25th February
When I started teaching one hour a week, I used to come home tired.  Then it went up to three hours and I came home knackered.  Now it’s five hours and I’m completely dead.  I did nothing all morning but at 1 o’clock I cycled into town to meet Rich.  I sorted out my bus ticket for when my parents come and we bought our train tickets for tomorrow’s Tokyo trip which came to just over £100 (see last entry).  We had McDonalds and then I cycled to Usui-San’s house to teach.  I got paid £30, got fed and a birthday present (and card which said ‘You are a good teacher, good friend and very special man for us’.  I was quite moved and quite speechless when I opened my crap present.  From 7-8 o’clock I taught Mr and Mrs Takizawa, the neighbours (but the kids were not there).  I came home and chatted to the Saiki’s about my movements in the near future.
