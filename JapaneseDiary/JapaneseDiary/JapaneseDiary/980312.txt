Thursday 12th March
Today we caught the train into Bandai again but this time went and explored the other side of the station.  We went to a few clothes shops to buy me some clothes for my birthday.  However, the jeans shops, although having a plethora of jackets and trousers had no matching sets (that’s weird) so we bought none there.  We headed back to the usual side of the station and had lunch in the same place as Monday.  I decided to just buy jean trousers cos I saw a pair I liked in Daiei and bought a shirt to go with it.  On the bottom floor I saw a pair of boots I liked which Mum and Dad bought me as a birthday present.  This is all part of my new Years resolution which was, since I have reached middle age this year, to change my image so that I am now the Marlboro man.  We went to a few more shops including the bookshop and I bought some stationary and some toothpaste (not from the bookshop).  Then I found a jacket to match the jeans and now I am finally a fully-fledged cowboy apart from the guns.  And the horse.  And the Stetson.  And the cowgirl wife.  However, I did meet a red Indian once.  We had coffee on the station before returning home in the rain.  We had to sit around then for a few hours more whilst she prepared dinner which I thoroughly enjoyed (the meal, that is, not the waiting) but I don’t think Mum and Dad did.  After the meal we chatted for an hour or two and Mrs Saiki gave Mum some gifts.  I paid for the rent with an extra £100 from Mum and Dad before coming up here.  Akihiro came in because he wants to give Mum and Dad a present in the morning.  I cracked a joke and we both smiled and felt good about stuff!!
