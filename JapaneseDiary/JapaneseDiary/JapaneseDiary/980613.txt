Saturday 13th June
Hideously hungover, I hid in my darkened room all day today with my sunglasses on and my duvet over my head.  At 6.30 A&R picked me up but not before Mrs Saiki, being the nicest she has ever been, packed me off with a bag of goodies to eat as we watched the football.  First stop was Pizza Hut.  It was a real treat.  Pizza here is very, very expensive but it was worth the money and the 30 minutes wait.  We took it back to International House and gorged ourselves into stupors, feeling sick all the way through Nigeria vs Spain (a great match and a shock that Nigeria were so good – very tight manoeuvres, keeping the pressure up constantly).  Korea vs Mexico was especially good due to the room being filled with Koreans and Mexicans.  After the dill Holland vs Belgium match I cam home to bed at 6am.
