Friday 12th December
Went into college at 2.30 and did some e-mailing.  Had law lecture.  Rich said his father’s company were having a year end party so we went into Bandai city where we got loads of free food and beer.  Spoke to R’s host mother’s younger sister about my date tomorrow and she gave me £25.  I don’t really understand why but it was very welcome.  We moved on to a bar in Furomachi.  There were beautiful girls there who were employed to chat to the customers.  It was quite cool.  One of them kept making me sing Beatles songs which I did willingly (it was a karaoke bar).  This is the first time I’ve been to a karaoke bar in Japan – usually we go to karaoke rooms where you pay just to sing and drink.  At this bar it was free and you got an audience which was cool – everyone loved me!  When R’s parents finally dragged us away they gave me and Balàzs £7.50 each to get a taxi but I decided to walk – it took one hour.
