Sunday 15th February
Spent another valentine-less day.  I went out the house once for an hour’s cycling in the afternoon.  I found out where I went wrong on 20th November when I got lost cycling home – apart from this brief excursion all I have done all day is read me book and study a little.  I’m so bored I’m lethargic.  I spent most of the day day-dreaming about the following:- 1) My parents coming to Japan in 3 weeks. 2) Meeting my friends again. 3) Living in Cardiff with Geoff, KT, Kester etc again. 4) Graduating, getting a cool job with a beautiful wife and an equally beautiful BMW.  Actually, something else did happen today, Mum and Dad phoned and we had a nice chat about stuff for 20 minutes.  They don’t seem very excited about coming, but I guess they’re just nervous about the flight, the Japanese and other unscary stuff.  Well, it’s about time I stopped writing this exciting entry and let you get on with picking your arse or whatever other exciting thing you could be doing in comparison.
