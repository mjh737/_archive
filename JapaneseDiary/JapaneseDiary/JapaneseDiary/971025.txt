Saturday 25th October
Went to some mountain with R’s host family.  We made the summit in about 90 mins.  I raced up whereas the Youngsters (R&B) lagged behind somewhat.  At the top the view was great but it started to rain so after lunch, we came down and bumped into Andy and Rachel coming up – how bizarre!  On the way back, I showed my age and fell asleep but I don’t think anyone noticed.  This evening I had a good chat with Mrs Saiki about nothing in particular for two hours and, although I tried to study, I ended up going to bed at 10 o’clock.  After that, I wrote my diary and, after that, I went to sleep.  When I woke up the next morning, it was…
