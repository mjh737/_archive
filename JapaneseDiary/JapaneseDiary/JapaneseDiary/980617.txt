Wednesday 17th June
My first good nights sleep for some time but I’m still exhausted.  This morning I studied a bit and read some more of ‘Degree of Guilt’.  After lunch I showered and got ready for my penultimate Usui visit.  I cycled there for there o’clock and taught Mitsuru for an hour.  When Yoshimi turned up she gave me a senbetsu which is a special kind of envelope, special in the sense that it contains money!!  I put it in my bag, unpoened, and continued the with the lesson.  However, none of us were really in the mood so we played Japanese chess instead.  At six o’clock I played Mario Kart with Ayumu and then realsed that Mrs Usui had cooked me dinner, thinking I was teaching her neighbours afterwards which I wasn’t.  An embarrassing situation arose and we both apologies.  She then gave me two presents which was very kind although I don’t want either of them.  I cam home, had dinner with Mr Saiki, walked Pao and came up to study and read.
