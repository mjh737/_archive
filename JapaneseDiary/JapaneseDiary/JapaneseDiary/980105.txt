Monday 5th January
Woke up late and went down for boiled egg.  It was heavenly but I could have used a cup of tea.  I’ve only had one cup since I’ve been here!  Came back to my room and finished reading East of Eden which ends with another death.  I can remember 22 deaths in the book and the last one hurt more than the others.  I studied loads and then took a break by going to the post office.  After tea, I took Pao for a walk and when I cam back I chatted with Mrs Saiki for 2 hours about Japanese and everything else under the (rising) sun.  Back in my room again I am sorting out all the grammar, vocab and kanji which I didn’t understand from the conversation and trying to translate a book about Ieyasu Tokugawa which I bought the other day to assist me in my dissertation.
