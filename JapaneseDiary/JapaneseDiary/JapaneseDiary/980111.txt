Sunday 11th January
Spent a couple of hours chatting to Mrs Saiki before catching the train to meet Rich at 1.30.  We waited for the girls for half an hour but they stood us up!  So, instead, we went to the American Diner and had a huge pizza each.  We moved on to Joypolis in a perfunctory manner and had coffee and a few games of Le Mans 24 before heading back to the station.  When I got home, I was still absolutely stuffed so I cancelled dinner and came up to study.  At 7.00 Mrs S called me downstairs to video the second part of a Japanese historical drama.  Apparently it’s going to last a whole year which means I’ll have to buy 25 videos if I want the lot.  I walked Pao and then came upstairs to study again.  I’ve just been reading more of my next Steinbeck novel, Cannery Row, which is pretty damn good so far.
