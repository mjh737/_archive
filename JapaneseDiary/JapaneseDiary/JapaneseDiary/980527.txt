Wednesday 27th May
Got up late, still knackered after a night of vivid dreams and nightmares about just about everything.  Studied some in the early afternoon.  I had only two pieces of toast for breakfast and nothing for lunch, not being abl to face the raw-looking cheap minced meat and decaying broccoli.  By the time I got to the Usui’s to teach I was starving and shaking but as usual got fed loads of kiddie food which made me feel slightly better.  They were all absolute horrors today and nothing was achieved.  I left at 6.15 after a few games of Mario Kart with Ayumu which, naturally, I won.  When I got home, nobody was in but I was relieved to find a small bow of spaghetti waiting for me which I consumed rapidly.  I then took Pao for a walk and bought some bread to have sandwiches in my room.  Now it is only 8.15 but in about an hour I shall go to bed.
