Friday 24th April
Sprang out of bed at 8.00 this morning like my bed was about to self-destruct.  By Zeus I don’t know why, it just happened.  Anyway I took the opportunity to eat breakfast and shower dead quick so I could get to college before Mrs Saiki arose.  I studied for a couple of hours and then had a sandwich for lunch, met Balàzs briefly to do some e-mailing before getting the train.  Although it has drizzled a bit from time to time today, it was the hottest day of the year by far – it was ridiculous.  Incidentally, on the subject of unusual phenomenon, last night at 12.00 I was sitting here studying when all of a sudden there was a loud bang, my pen flew out of my hand and I fell over (from a sitting position!!).   I lay on the floor panting for a few seconds with the house shaking around me and threatening to fall down.  It was only a bloody grade four earthquake!!  It was very exciting due to the adrenalin and I hope it happens again, but worse.
