Wednesday 14th January
Got up late and studied until 2pm.  Received a letter from Rob about 5 minutes after thinking I ought to write to him.  The thing which reminded me to write was a man outside fondling a long rubber tube near the roadworks!!  I went to my students house for 3pm and for the first hour taught the 6 year old son who can barely speak Japanese but it was a challenge and enjoyable.  From 4-6, I taught Ayumu and his girlfriend.  Basically I just rambled on at them for two hours until Mrs Usui gave me some money (£30-) upon which I cycled home in the snow.  We had gyōza for tea and when there was one left, the three of us ‘boys’ caught each others’ eyes and smiled.  I’ve never seen Akihiro smile before.  After dinner I chatted to Yoshitaka for a bit before coming up to study and read.
