Sunday 16th November
I actually went out the house today and, what’s more, I wore my suit!  We met at 10.30 and went to a tea ceremony.  It was all a bit commercial but very interesting and very beautiful.  In fact, I am in love with one of the tea-ceremoniers.  The second of the three we saw was a debut ceremony for one of the girls.  The whole ceremony was perfect, very elegant.  She got up to leave and farted.  No, not really, only joking, but she did manage to crack her head on the bit above the door and everyone laughed at her, poor wee lassie.  When she gracefully re-entered, one Nippo woman couldn’t suppress her amusement and just burst out laughing, immediately followed by all the rest of us failed suppressors.  Afterwards went to McDonalds for lunch and then onto Sega Rally, Virgin and book shop.  This evening I took the dog for a run and studied a little but now I think I will have an early night.
