Monday 30th March
I tried to get up at 8 o’clock but failed and slept in until 10 o’clock.  I pottered around until 3.30, studying and reading.  I got a parcel from Mum including the letter I asked her to type for Allen and Overy which had a spelling mistake so I phoned to ask for a revised copy.  Also got letters from Katy and Nan.  I caught the train into town to meet Balàzs, Miyako and Zita and after a brief trip to the Sega place, we did the usual and went to the American Diner for food and beer.  We played drinking games but Balàzs and I drank less than usual.  After that we did karaoke for a couple of hours but I wasn’t quite as brilliant as I usually am.  After karaoke I should have got the train home but we all convinced each other that it would be a great idea to watch the sun come up over the river so we got beer and food supplies in and set off in search of a vantage point.  The girls were soon defeated by the cold and went home saying they will find us later and Balàzs and I carried on until we found a big fishing boat to sit in.  We chatted and drank for a couple of hours until Balàzs fell asleep.  By this time it was very very cold and I couldn’t sleep so I walked up and down for half an hour before returning to the boat to find Balàzs snoring heavily amongst a pile of old rope.  I laid down among some floats and curled up in a ball and actually managed to sleep a little.  When I awoke, Balàzs wasn’t there so I got my stuff together and got off the boat.  Balàzs came back and said he’d found a hotel that was open.  It was the Niigata Grand and it was open and warm so we explored the various conference rooms etc.  Later we went outside again for 2 more hours before the girls turned up, much to our surprise.  We watched a rather pathetic sunrise before going home.  I crawled into bed at 6.45.
