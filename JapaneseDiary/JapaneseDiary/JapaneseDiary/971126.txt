Wednesday 26th November
Got up at 10 o’clock and honestly did try to eat my cold eggs and meat balls but unfortunately failed.  Pao, however, passed the test.  Wrote my law report until lunch and then went for a short bike ride down to the beach and back.  After that I finished my report in rough before starting to write it up.  After dinner I walked the beast and here I am now at 8.45.  It’s so boring sometimes.  There is absolutely nothing to do and the only people to do it with are B & R.  It’s days like this when I learn nothing new and just want to come home.  I chatted to Mrs Saiki for a bit about my difficulties and she told me I was too intelligent and therefore think too much rather than relaxing and trying to understand.  She’s probably right, I do think far too much about what I hear, but how do I stop?
