Friday 20th February
We got thrown out of the youth hostel at 10.00.  It was actually more of a palace than a youth hostel – private room with two beds, shower, coffee, balcony et cetera.  We decided to go into central Tokyo before going home in order to have breakfast.  We had coffee and sandwiches next to the Imperial Grounds.  It started to rain so Andy and Rachel wanted to stay there while me and Alex, Rachel’s (now ex) boyfriend tried to see the Imperial Palace but it’s shut on Fridays!! We got the train back to the station near where we parked the car and after briefly getting lost and being helped by an armed policeman, we eventually got back to the restaurant and, to our surprise, the car was still there!!
We drove back in the rain with Rachel driving.  Andy and I did a crossword in the back.  We stopped for dinner and realised we hadn’t filled up with petrol since leaving Niigata!!  I also realised it was dark and I had told my host mother that I would be back in the morning so I had to make a hasty phonecall.  We finally got back home at 8 o’clock and I had a brief conversation with my host mother who is off to Tokyo herself for a few days tomorrow.  I came up here, unpacked and went to bed to read for an hour before going to sleep at about 10 o’clock.
