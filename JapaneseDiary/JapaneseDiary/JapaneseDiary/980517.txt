Sunday 17th May
Today was very very hot and humid and along with the fact that I couldn’t sleep because of hayfever and the fact that I had a slight hangover, I was lethargic all day and didn’t do anything at all.  Misa phoned about lunch time and I will go and see them next Sunday if I can get a bus ticket.  The weekend after I’m going to Dom’s and the weekend after that he will probably come here.  After that there will only be three weeks until I go home.  Today is the 5/6 mark which makes it seem like there is still ages to go but there’s not really.  Now it’s getting close to my return I’m starting to feel even sadder because although I am generally unhappy it has become my life and it’s going to hurt.  Anyway, I walked Pao before being forced to watch a program about English botanists.  Now I’m going to get an early night.
