Monday 22nd December
Went into college to meet my meek personal tutor but we actually chatted quite well this time but only about Japanese grammar.  After that I met Yō my high school girl and made her tell me her life story by pretending it would be very good for her English.  After that I had to rush home for my year’s end party with my host brother.  It was just the two of us.  Mr and Mrs Saiki had gone out to proper Bōnenkai parties and she asked me to come home early for a Bōnenkai with Akihiro.  Basically, all that happened was we ordered Miso Rāmen from the local restaurant and I bought some beer in an attempt at male bonding.  Akihiro was too scared to drink a lot in case his mum came back!  Anyway, we sat in near silence for 4½ hours before mum came back and broke up the party.
