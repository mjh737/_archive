Wednesday 4th February
Finally I’ve had my haircut.  The first time in Japan (it was getting a little shaggy at the back).  It was a nightmare trying to explain what I wanted and the pictures were all of Japanese 1980’s (i.e. current) haircuts but I got there and am quite pleased with the finished product.  Balàzs and I had a beer afterwards to relieve our stress and then I headed off to my students’ house.  Had quite a good lesson and cracked a few jokes with Ayumu who is really quite good at the old speaking English thing.  Cycled home, laden with ¥6000, and almost froze my hands off.  When I got back Mrs S was out and there was no dinner or bread so Mr S let me walk to the shop and buy some so I could have toast for dinner.  I walked the dog, studied and slept.
