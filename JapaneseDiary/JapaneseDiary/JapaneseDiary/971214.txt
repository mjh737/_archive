Sunday 14th December
Woke up at Miho’s and we took tea together before I went home.  On the way (I was cycling) a car came tearing up a side road straight into me.  In Japan pedestrians and cyclists have the right of way in such situations.  Anyway, he crashed into me, flattening my bike and sending me flying Bruce Lee style across the main road.  I was okay apart from a pulled muscle.  I was going to hurl all sorts of vulgarities at him but instead shook his hand and told him not to worry.  The Saiki’s told me I should have reported him to the police.  At 5.00 I cycled to Balàzs and we went to the GEOS language school with his host brother.  It was very expensive but a good time was had by all.  We played a game whereby each group had to do certain things like find someone with a lipstick and all put it on.  Balàzs and I went to the next place, already drunk, drunk more and then went to another place and drunk more. On the way home I fell off my bike and lost the lock but I can’t remember which way I went!!
