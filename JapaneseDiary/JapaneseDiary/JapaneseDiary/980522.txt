Friday 22nd May
It was one of those funny days that lasts for ages and seems to have been an expedition through life rather than just a day.  The morning and afternoon were boring and I just studied etc and at 6.30 I met Rich to go to uni and together we walked to the designated meeting place for the tutors party.  The actual party was okay – beer and food but afterwards we went to Mashies.  I hadn’t been there for ages and it was good.  We played every drinking game possible simultaneously before, at 2.30 deciding to go to the beach.  So, about 8 of us went to the sea to swim.  Andy and Meiko, at one stage alone in the sea together, became slightly closer than is reasonably allowable whilst in a relationship with another and, subsequently, Rachel filed for divorce.  I, on the other hand, had a great swim and got home at 5.30am on....
