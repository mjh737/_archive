Wednesday 28th January
I’ve just finished my secret bedroom snack of jam sandwiches and grapefruit juice – yum yum.  It’s now 10 o’clock and I shall be off to bed soon.  Today I studied all morning and at 2.30 left for my 3 hour session with my students.  Got there 10 minutes late due to the snow and after 3 hours of amusement but headache, I took my £30 and left.  Cycling home took an hour cos it was a sheet of ice.  I almost fell asleep in the longe before dinner.  Mr Saiki asked me his weekly “Is Cardiff cold?” question to make conversation and I replied with my weekly “Yes”.  I walked the hound again and escaped up here to revise for the kanji test and to have my feast.  I’m just off to bed now with my Steinbeck novel, Tortilla Flat.
