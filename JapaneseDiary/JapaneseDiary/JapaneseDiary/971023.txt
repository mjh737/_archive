Thursday 23rd October
It’s Saturday now and I really have no idea what I did during the day on Thursday but, after college, we did karaoke, us and about 5 Nihonjin, and Balàzs and I became steadily inebriated as usual.  There’s this really cool and happy Jap song which I keep singing but can’t remember the name of.  I’ll have to find out. After karaoke we moved on to a pub and I ordered some food.  After paying, I was presented with a laminated receipt and a compact plastic black box which didn’t look anything like I was expected from the picture on the menu.  Five or ten minutes later, a miniature siren started up from within said black box and I assumed, correctly, that this was my cue to collect my meal.
After that, feeling rather the worse for wear, I returned home.
