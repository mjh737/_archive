Friday 5th June
Hungover, tired and lonely I spent the day in bed reading my new Richard North Patterson book which Andy lent me last night.  I did pop to uni very briefly to return a library book and in that brief moment I bumped into Balàzs who was in uni for the same reason.  We walked back to the station together then I cycled home.  I read a bit more before Mrs Saiki came home to tell me she was going out.  She came back an hour later and we commenced consumption of a rather bland meal of soiled salmon and this frothy component which Pao may well have eaten at some stage last week when he was ill and throwing up all the time.  I then proceeded to walk the said regurgitater before sneaking back upstairs to read.  I’m too tired to do anything really so I shall probably sleep instead.
