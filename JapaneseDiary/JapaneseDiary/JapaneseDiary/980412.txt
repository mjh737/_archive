Sunday 12th April
Woke up with a hangover and was taken straight out to do cherry blossom viewing which is a Japanese concept entailing sitting beneath a tree and getting pissed.  We have the same thing in England but without the tree.  Actually it was the last thing I wanted but it actually cured my hangover.  When I got back Mrs Saiki hung all my bedding off the balcony to air the mould stains and decaying threads – a pointless action methinks.  I cycled to meet Andy and Rich at uni in order to play football but, once we realised that we were either mad dogs or English man on this ridiculously sweltering day, we decided the Japanese were actually on to a good thing with regard to the tree/beer combo so we had our own ‘hanami’ party until about 8 o’clock and much beer was consumed by all.  Andy and I then went back to the international house where I had the most advanced conversation about philosophy with a Polish guy (an El Savadorian and a Brazilian) before cycling home and falling asleep, clothed, on the floor cos my sheets were still not on the bed.
