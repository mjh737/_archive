Sunday 4th January
Woke up with a ‘weltschmerz’ which is, unfortunately, not a Thai princess but, I believe, is the sadness you get some days for no apparent reason.  I tried to discover what was causing the pain and can’t believe it was just last nights over-charging incident.  When Mr & Mrs Saiki came home at 6 o’clock, I told them and they were so nice and helpful, I almost cried.  They told me I should have let the barman call the police and then I should have called the Saiki’s.  Hopefully it won’t happen again.  I learnt that the Japanese actually have a word which means ‘overcharge a Westerner’.  I went into town to meet Balàzs at 9 and we played a few games of Sega Rally and went to McDonalds.  I caught the train home at 11 o’clock and read East of Eden until 2am.  I’ve nearly finished it.  Currently I have two lives – Niigata, Japan and Salinas, California. They are both loved but, by both, I am also deeply saddened.
