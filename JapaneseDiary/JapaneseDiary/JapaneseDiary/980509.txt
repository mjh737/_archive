Saturday 9th May
This morning was dull and so was the afternoon.  I should have done my dissertation but couldn’t muster up the willpower.  Instead, I read my book.  At 3pm I went into town to meet R&B who I haven’t seen for over a week.  We decided, well, I decided, to avoid the American Diner because it would have been ‘uncool’ to see Yūmi.  So we went to Magic City and had a few games of Sega Rally before moving to the bar section where we consumed draught bass which was different and pleasant.  We had a sub-standard sandwich before playing pool for an hour.  We had a few more beers before splitting up.  Rich went on a hunt for more food and Balàzs and I went looking for more beer.  We found a little bar and had another drink before catching the last train home.  I went to seven eleven and bought some supplies for my room.  When I got back, I realised I’d lost my key.  I panicked and went and sat in the school field to ponder my predicament.  Having concluded that sleeping under a tree just wasn’t going to work, I broke in through the bathroom window in true ninja style and got to bed at 1.15am.
