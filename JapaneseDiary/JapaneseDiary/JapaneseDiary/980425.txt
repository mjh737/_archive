Saturday 25th April
I went into town to meet Balàzs and Rich at 3 o’clock.  We went to Magic City for a bit.  It’s the new Joypolis but it’s not that good.  We went back to the station at 5pm to meet up with Tatsuru, his girlfriend and Miyako.  We went to the usual and stayed there until 9 o’clock upon which we moved next door for a couple of hours to do karaoke.  After that I tried to persuade Rich and Balàzs to go to the second Gaikin party in Furumachi but they were both too sad and did not (Balàzs because of Miyako and Rich, because of his host mother).  So I got a little unhappy but, mentioning in passing the party to Tatsuru on the way back to the station, I was surprised to get an affirmative answer so the three of us turned round and hiked back over the bridge to the party.  We bought our drinks and stood around for 15 minutes.  Tatsuru grabbed some Japanese girl and tried to get her to talk to me but that one fell through.  However, I did see the girl from Maryland who I met last Saturday so I chatted to her for a bit and found myself engaged in dance with her.  However Tatsuru broke it up to tell me that I owed him money which I didn’t but I lent home some anyway and he went home at 12.30.  Next I bumped into Lee or Li or whatever her name is, the Chinese girl I went out with with Balàzs in January-ish.  We danced for a bit and then I saw a Japanese girl I knew and she introduced me to some other Japs who I chatted to for hours and gave my phone number to but I hope they don’t ring.  At 5.00am the party finished and I went to the Royal Host for an hour with two Canadian, two Americans, a Jap and a Scottish girl and had breakfast.  I finally got home and went to bed at 7.30.
