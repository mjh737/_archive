Thursday 27th November
Went to college, did some e-mail, finished my report for tomorrow.  That’s it, one line.  I’ve done nothing else apart from eat dinner which took 30 mins as the Saiki’s are out.  I’m so bored –I got home at 12 and it’s 9 now.  What can I do?  I bought a can of beer from over the road which I’ve almost finished so I may buy another.  I’m really only still in Japan because it’s part of my degree, but I’ve had enough – the novelty has worn off and I’m bored because I have no real friends.  Hopefully I will make some but the language barrier is a real problem.  I’m in my room, there is nothing else to do at all, I don’t know anyone nearby although I’d love to go out.  If anyone has any ideas let me know, although I will be home when you read this.  I wonder how I will rate this year in Japan when I do get home.  As far as I can see now, it can only get better.
