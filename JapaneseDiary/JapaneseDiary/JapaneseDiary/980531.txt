Sunday 31st Mary 
Everyone sprang awake and the two Japs walked us to the station for 7am.  Said goodbye to Tamsin and Rich and I got the train to Ikeburo (about an hour away).  We sat in McDonalds for a bit feeling god-damned awful.  When the bus came I thought I’d chuck but I survived and by the time we got to Niigata at 2pm I was fine.  We had lunch in Furumachi before walking back to Bamdai and doing a bit of shopping.  I got the train home, spoke to Mrs Saiki for 5 minutes and then came up here for a little peace and quiet.  Dinner was gyoza, big and nice.  I walked Pao afterwards.  When I got back Mrs Saiki tried to make a joke but I just couldn’t understand and apologised before coming up here completely shattered.  Went to bed at about 11pm.
