Saturday 27th December
Woke up late and got up even later since I decided to stay in bed and read East of Eden which is incredible.  In the afternoon I was all set to see Naoki but when Mrs Saiki phoned him, he was not there so, instead, she took me into Niigata to do New Year shopping but, in the usual Saiki manner, it was the wrong day, it starts tomorrow!  So instead, we walked around the market for a bit and then popped in to the international friendship centre just to look but Mrs Saiki got talking to some woman who wants an English tutor for her son (10 years old).  Contract signed there and then – 1-2 hrs, once a week, £10 an hour.  Driving home, Mrs Saiki stopped off at her shrine and did some weird Jappo clapping/praying stuff.  I asked her to point out Naoki’s house since we were in the area and I wasn’t sure.  She made me go in and arrange tomorrow.  He had company.  A girl who is studying in America.  Good English but ugly and I had to give her my number cos she’s keen to meet soon.  Mrs Saiki said ‘that was lucky’ – yeah right.
