Wednesday 21st January
Got up at 10.30 for breakfast.  Miho phoned because she wanted to cancel our cinema trip tonight, or rather postpone it until Monday, because she has a headache.  Likely story, probably something better came up like an offer for her to have needles stuck in her eyes.  At 2.15 I cycled to my students house and Mrs Usui said, in English, that from March I can have another student (her neighbour) afterwards from 7-8pm and that I should meet them tonight.  So, after the lesson which was okay and easy money, I hung around and she brought me a drink and said since I’m hanging around, can I play with the kids for an extra ¥2000 so I did and then had dinner.  When I asked when we would meet the neighbours, she said not tonight.  I don’t understand what went wrong but I got ¥2000 and dinner and a little embarrassment out of it before cycling home.
