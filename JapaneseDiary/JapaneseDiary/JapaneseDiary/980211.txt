Wednesday 11th February
It’s a national holiday today so I had to eat my cold greasy eggs in front of Mr Saiki and look like I was enjoying them.  I felt sick for an hour afterwards.  I spent the morning working out my timetable for the next month and for when Mum and Dad come.  I presented these to the Saikis who didn’t really make much of a comment – I think they are bored of me now and want me out – still only four and a half months left.  Went to see my students from 3-6 and progress was definitely made.  I quite enjoyed it but it’s very tiring and very hard work.  The major difference between teaching kids and adults is for kids you can’t write things down or talk about grammar.  Still, it’s a challenge.  I came home, had dinner and came up here and did absolutely nothing due to lethargy.  I will go to bed now and try to read some of my Japanese history book to inspire me with regard to my dissertation.
