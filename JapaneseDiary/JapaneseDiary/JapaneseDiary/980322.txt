Sunday 22nd March
Mrs Saiki has gone away to some unspoken destination for a few days – this means food (amongst other things) misery.  Had a bit of toast for breakfast and then, having left a note for Mr Saiki saying if I’m not back by 7.00 then I am eating out, I went into Furumachi to meet Rich.  We had a coffee before going to the cinema to see James Bond which, contrary to all expectations, was pretty damn good.  I think I might be James Bond after uni.  Had a quick look in a few shops and bought a cheap Grishamesque Japanese novel on the station.  Got back at 6.55 shortly followed by Mr Saiki who had gone out at 6.30 to buy some food for him and Akihiro.  He kindly gave me a small bowl of cold rice.  So I walked Pao, bought bread and had marmite sarnies in my room while reading my new book.
