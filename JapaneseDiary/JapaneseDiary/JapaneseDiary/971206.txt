Saturday 6th December
Woke up with Meiko and a terrifying hangover.  She nursed me a little which was nice.  After an hour of coffee and a sandwich, we piled into T’s car and he drove to Miyako’s house which took about 45 mins.  B & Miyako got out there and the three of us, me, T & Miyako, went on to Niigata uni station.  By this stage we’d been in the car over an hour and I was on the verge of blowing chunks (reminds me of a joke!).  However, Meiko delicately cradled me in her arms and, whilst stroking my hair, informed me that deep down inside, however old, men are all children.  Too true.  I was a mess anyway and we had to keep stopping but we finally made it.  I cycled home and went to bed.  Had dinner, walked Pao and then had my third Jappo bath – sugoi, ne.
