Wednesday 19th November
Wednesday’s are free now so I stayed home all day reading/studying and rearranging my room yet again.  I went out to buy envelopes but they had none at ‘Save On’ so I cycled to the next branch 100 meters down the road.  There must be 20 branches between here and uni!  After dinner I walked man’s best friend (yeah right) again and have just finished studying again.  I decided today to write a book.  I’ve not planned it yet, have no idea what it’s going to be about but have started it nonetheless.  I’m just going to write and see what happens – actually I’ll probably throw it away tomorrow and conclude it was just another of my hair-brained fads.  That reminds me, Ludlow hasn’t written yet.  Neither have Emma, Hunt and Kelehar.  Well at least I know who my real friends are – Grandad and the bank!!
