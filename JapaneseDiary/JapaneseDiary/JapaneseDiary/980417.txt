Friday 17th April
Laid in until 10.30 and went down to breakfast which to my surprise, and extreme delight, was a box of cereal.  It was absolutely heavenly and I desire similar consumption tomorrow morning.  I met Rich on the train and we did our usual twice a week tour of Bandai.  We had a McDonalds lunch (that’s two good meals so far, what is lined up for dinner I wondered).  I bought the complete Japanese law book in Japanese which was (ridiculously) only £5.  It’s a bit heavy going though with little dialogue, well, none actually.  Came home and, for dinner, was shocked to find my favourite meal – gyōza, which are little spring roll kind of things with a kind of green goo inside – so that’s 3 great meals in a twelve hour period.  Walked the vicious wolf-dog and bought beer on the way back to celebrate Friday night in true style.  Spent half an hour in the living room not talking or being talked to by Mrs Saiki and now here I am ready for bed at 9.30pm.  Goodnight.
