Tuesday 24th March
Got up nice and early and cycled into college to e-mail Kate, Geoff and Casper.  Rich and I went to the rāmen place for lunch and then I cycled home.  All afternoon I did kanji.  Mrs Saiki came home today – she’s been away at some three-day play performed by a travelling company in the country.  Due to her presence, the food situation improved 20-fold and I even gave her a hearty smile when I saw her which she returned by express post.  We had a little chat about stuff and then I walked the canine killer pungent-smelling boot-recipient.  I had a nice wander along the river and dreamt of being in the lounge at Cardiff.  Came back and wrote to Katie with pen and paper for a change and enclosed my tenancy agreement for next year.  I studied a bit more and prepared for tomorrows trip to Sadō Island which is still on at present – third time lucky.
