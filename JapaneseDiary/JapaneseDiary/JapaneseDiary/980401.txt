Wednesday 1st March April (What went wrong there!!)
I got up at 10 o’clock and, after breakfast, I decided that today was to be a highly successful today.  I had 4½ hours to kill before teaching so I made a plan, stuck to it and ended up sorting out my entire life, writing countless letters and generally feeling more confident about my future.  I’ve made a ‘life plan’ which, if I carry it out fully and correctly, will result in my being very happy, successful, intelligent and likable.  Tomorrow, however, I will probably be suicidal with misery again, wallowing in self-pity and lethargy.  Today though I am ‘King of the World’ and my room is my oyster and maybe other places if I get the opportunity to see them.  I cycled to my students in the pleasant sunshine, taught for 5 hours and then cycled home in the torrential rain, freezing and soaking.  I am still feeling positive though, much to my surprise.  I chatted to Mr and Mrs Saiki (at least, as much as they were willing to allow) and now I am ready for bed.
