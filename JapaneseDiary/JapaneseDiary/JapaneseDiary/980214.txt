Saturday 14th February
Woke up to the sound of pouring rain and decided just to lay in bed and be miserable for another hour.  Today is Valentines day and I haven’t even received any post for a week, let alone a VD card (there must be grounds for a joke in that abbreviation).  What is cool about today, though, is it’s exactly halfway through my sentence – 137 days here, 137 to go.  All I did on this momentous occasion was cycle to a bargain shop and buy some new pads, shoelaces and video tapes.  The rest of the day I’ve just read and studied.  Yesterday I was at an all time low – things can only get better but I can’t see how.  I’m now content to be on my own.  I don’t want to go out or see/speak to anyone.  I just want to curl up in a ball and let the time pass.
