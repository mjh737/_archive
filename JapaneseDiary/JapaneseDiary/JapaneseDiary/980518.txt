Monday 18th May
Today was to be busy but not as busy as it actually turned out.  I got up at 8.30 and went to college for my lecture at 10.30.  At 12.00 I met the Japanese students who are coming to Cardiff for half an hour next year (you may wish to contemplate that sentence for a while.)  I did e-mailing for a bit after that and then went for rāmen with Rich, Andy and Rachel. Rich and I went into town after lunch and I bought my bus tickets for the Misa, Yuri and Naoko weekend.  We met up with A&R again for a drink in Media Bar and I chatted to my Chinese friend, Li, for about 20 minutes.  At six o’clock we went to Magic City and had a few beers and played pool and Sega Rally for a few hours.  Rich only lasted 30 minutes before going home to his host mum.  A&R&I went to the diner for a meal and some more beer and an argument about the merits of our respective genders before catching the last train home.
