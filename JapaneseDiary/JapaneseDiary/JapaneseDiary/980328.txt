Saturday 28th March
I’ve just gone over the road to buy a beer (today is a different day to yesterday – yesterday I also bought beer.  The beer seems to assist me in slipping into a world of my own and while away the long boring nights when I should be out with my friends in TW/C.  I am feeling really down today – lonely and sad and bored.  The days are dragging on at the moment cos there really is nothing to do apart from buy stuff which, believe it or not, costs money.  All I can remember doing today was going on a 30 minutes bike ride merely to buy food to make up for the inadequate Saiki lunch thing I had at, strangely, lunchtime.  There are now 95 days left which means I’ve been here an unhealthy length of time.  At least there is a light at the end of the tunnel now but as Mr Saiki pointed out, Japan has the longest tunnels in the world.  I wish I could get through it a little faster.
