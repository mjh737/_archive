Saturday 11th October
Richard phoned early this morning to invite me to the hot springs but, since it involves getting your kit off with a load of Japanese gangsters, I gratefully declined. I studied for a couple of hours and tried to figure out where all my money has gone.  After an unsuccessful attempt at both, I decided to go for a bike ride.  I saw the mountains after only a few minutes and headed towards them.  An hour and a half later I was there; mountains to the left of me, sea to the right, here I am stuck in the middle of an unearthly rain storm in t-shirt and tracksuit trousers.  I was soaked and cold and not looking forward to my 1 ½ hour return journey.  Fortunately though, I discovered that you can get hot cocoa out of the drinks machines that adorn the roadsides of every street, big or small.  Now, I am home, and as it is 8 o’clock, I must bid you ‘goodnight’. Goodnight!
