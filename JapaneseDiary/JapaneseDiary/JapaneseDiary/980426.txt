Sunday 26th April
Having gone to bed at 7.30am, I was very tired at 11.00 when I got up but I didn’t feel too tired.  Had a bit of a weltschmerz but cannot find the reason.  I managed to go down to have some toast but then came back up and laid in bed all afternoon reading.  Dom phoned in the afternoon and said that Debbie had been deported for some reason or other – taihen desu ne.  I’ve agreed with Dom that I will go down to Tokyo and see him for a weekend probably in May.  I’m looking forward to it.  Mum and Dad phoned at 7pm and we had a nice chat – I want to go home really badly now.  Only 66 days to go.  I had dinner and a slightly better than average chat with the Saikis before walking the dog and coming up here to write my diary.  Mr Saiki has just brought my dictionaries up which I left downstairs earlier – it was very kind of him and therefore totally out of character.
