Wednesday Friday 3rd March April (completely wrong!!)
 Where did that come from.  I spent all day studying and actually learnt quite a lot of useful stuff.  Went into town to meet everyone, including Zita, Andy and Rachel and we did the usual American Diner thing which really is the cheapest place for beer in town and the food, although on the pricey side, is the best in Niigata if not the whole of this Godforsaken country. Anyway once we had realised that no one could actually remember where the club was, we asked a few people and staggered around a bit, we eventually got into the club and lost the Americans.  It was quite rubbish and I didn’t really enjoy myself and afterwards I walked to Hakusan Station with A&R but couldn’t face hanging around in the cold for 3 hours so I walked home and crawled into bed at 3.45.
