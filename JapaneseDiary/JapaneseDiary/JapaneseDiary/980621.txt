Sunday 21st June
After breakfast at 12 o’clock I studied and read all afternoon until Mum and Dad phoned at 7pm.  It was good to speak to them and it is the last time I will do so before I see them in 10 days.  I had dinner straight away after that.  Unfortunately it was cold because it had been on the table throughout the twenty minute conversation with Mum and Dad.  I walked Pao and then explained that I have a Japanese exam tomorrow and came upstairs straight away.  However all I have done so far is finish my book.  It is now almost ten o’clock and I still have to revise for the exam, the result of which will be sent in a report to Cardiff.  Also I have to do at least an hour on my dissertation because my library book has to be back tomorrow.  I’ve got so much to do tomorrow during the day but when it’s over I just have the second England match to look forward to.
