Thursday 18th December
Alarm went off at six for me to study but I couldn’t handle it so I had another hour and sacrificed my shower instead.  Today was the end of term kanji test – I think I did okay.  After the lesson I used the computer but cos it’s Christmas received no mail from Cardiff.  Then Miho tapped me on the shoulder which was a pleasant surprise.  We went for a coffee and then back to the library to study where, upon her request, I wrote her a letter including a guess at exactly what our relationship is.  I look forward to her reply but she’s going to Kyūshū on Saturday for four days so it may be a long time coming.  Rich wasn’t too pleased that I went because we had planned to meet Rimi (see 16th Dec) for lunch, but as it turned out I was back in time anyway.  So we did Rāmen for lunch and then I went back to the library all afternoon to read Mice and Men and hope that an admirer would approach me but none did.  At home, I managed to escape early but Mr Saiki chased me and gave me a Japanese kids book which will actually be quite useful.
