Friday 6th February
Got up late and studied a bit.  At 1.30 I cycled to Balàzs’ to meet him and Miyako.  We went to the supermarket and bought some lunch and a shed load of beer.  We sat in his room and consumed the lot.  Then, with Hisaei, we walked into town to get to the American Diner for Happy Hour which lasts until 7pm.  I am in love, no honestly, with one of the waitresses – she’s beautiful.  I’ve seen her a few times before but it didn’t really sink in before.  Anyway, back to the plot – Hisaei phoned Sumiyo who somehow joined us 3 minutes later – I still can’t understand how.  We finished off our 5 pitchers of beer and went next door to do karaoke.  I wasn’t as good as usual due to alcohol consumption but I tried hard.  Afterwards I wanted to go back to American Diner but no one else did so I had to go home miserable and lonely.  I cycled thinking, as usual, that I wasn’t drunk but, of course, I was.
