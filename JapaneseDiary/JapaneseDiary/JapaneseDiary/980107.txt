Wednesday 7th January
Got up late as usual and did breakfast and shower.  I tried to study most of the day but was not really in the mood.  At 4pm Mrs Usui picked me up and took me to her house to teach her son English which I did for exactly one hour, after which she came up and said, “Okay, thank you, here’s your money”.  So that was easy enough.  They invited me to dinner and fed me some English food with Apple Pie and English Tea.  I managed to escape at 7.00 and after chatting to Mrs Saiki for an hour and walking Pao in the snow, I am now in my room.  Every night it’s been snowing but in the morning sun it melts so it’s not very deep yet.  I think February is the deep snow month and I look forward to it.  I got letters today from Nan and Mum and Dad and Miho (a postcard).  She claims she hadn’t contacted me before because she lost my address and phone number.  I don’t know if I believe her but, since she bothered to write, I’ll give her the benefit of the doubt.
