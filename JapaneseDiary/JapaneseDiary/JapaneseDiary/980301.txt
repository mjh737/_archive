Sunday 1st March
Got up at 11.30 but Mrs Saiki was not yet awake so I made breakfast and came back.  An hour later I went down for a shower and just as I was about to derobe, a voice from the bathroom alerted me to the fact that it was already occupied.  I had to wait but afterwards Mrs Saiki made me some lunch and we had a chat for about an hour.  I then came upstairs for a few more hours, venturing downstairs once, for coffee, and finding her asleep again.  I studied and sat around until dinner and then Mum and Dad phoned to confirm our meeting on Saturday.  They want to go to Sadō Island now so that’s another transportation nightmare for me to sort out.  I watched ‘Twister’ tonight which was good, if not over the top.  Now I shall read a little before going to bed.
