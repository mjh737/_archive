Thursday 8th January
Had to get up at 7.00 this morning.  There was five centimetres of snow so Mrs S gave me a lift to the university but because I was so tired, I skipped college and went to the library to do e-mail and get my next Steinbeck fix – Cannery Row.  B and R and I went into Niigata in order to meet Miyako and have Rāmen for lunch.  Rich and I went shopping afterwards and I bought an abacus.  We looked around in the bookshop and the department stores but no further purchases were made.  I came home and read a bit.  I went down at seven for dinner and Akihiro had ordered pizza for the two of us but by the time it got here, he was in bed so I ate my half alone.  I walked Pao in the snow (10cms has fallen in the last two hours and it’s still snowing).  Mrs Saiki tried to explain the abacus but I couldn’t understand.  I’m going to have to practicing so good night.
