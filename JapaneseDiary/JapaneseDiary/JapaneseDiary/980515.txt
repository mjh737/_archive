Friday 15th May
A nothing day.  I didn’t go out apart from walking Pao.  I didn’t get any post.  I just sat in my room and studied quite hard for my dissertation.  Nobody phoned, although I expected Balàzs to give me a call about going out so I guess I’m not going out tomorrow either.  I can’t phone him because he hasn’t given me Miyako’s new number.  Apart from studying I successfully completed a Telegraph crossword, read a little and spent a couple of hours staring at Yūmi in the photos I got back yesterday – sad but true.  There are 47 days to go now and I have bought a beer to celebrate this wondrous occasion.  The nearer I get to going home the more I am worried about what an anticlimax it will be.  Although I am unhappy 90% of the time here, it has been my life for the last 7 ½ months and I am quite attached to various things here (no, not just Yūmi!!).  I have a feeling it’s going to take a while for me to readjust to England.  Balàzs has since phoned and he and I shall be going out tomorrow night.
