Saturday 15th November
Today I feel much better.  Got up at 10.30 and had breakfast and a shower.  Mrs Saiki said there was a film on about Wales at 12.00 which turned out to be ‘The Englishman Mountain Hill Thing’ which was fairly average but the first film I’ve seen here.  Balàzs was supposed to ring at about 12 o’clock but didn’t until 5 o’clock so I had to stay in all day.  So, again all I have done is study and finish Marabou Stork Nightmares which was excellent.  Now I am reading Irvine Welsh’s other novel, Ecstasy, in Japanese.  I’m sure I write this diary more than once a day.  I’m always writing it.  Really I should just have kept writing from day one, constantly but all I would be able to write is “Wrote my diary, wrote my diary, wrote my diary” ad infinitum.
