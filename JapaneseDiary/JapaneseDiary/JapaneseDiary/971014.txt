Tuesday 14th October
Major hangover.  Painus maximus.  The Japs call it futsukayoi which means two days of drink or somethink.  Lets hope their not underestimating the duration.  Had a lecture from this guy called Nakamura Sensei.  He is incredible.  He spent his whole life working for a bank which sent him to foreign countries to learn the language and then build a branch.  He speaks more languages than there actually are and rarely makes a mistake.  He is worthy of respect more than anyone I’ve ever met before.
This morning was Jap test.  It took half an hour and wasn’t too taxing.  This evening I walked the dog, Pao, for an hour and got attacked by a huge owl which followed me for 10 minutes, swooping at my head.  This country is mad!!
